# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Copy-paste LinkedIn posts to X. **Why:** Format mismatch; engagement dies; looks bot-like to dev followers.
- **Don't:** Join engagement pods or "mutual RT" Discords. **Why:** Fake reach, wrong followers, platform risk; dev audience detects pods quickly.
- **Don't:** Thread 15+ tweets for a minor feature. **Why:** Completion rate collapses; thread gets truncated in feeds.
- **Don't:** Quote-tweet with "PLEASE RT" or tag 20 accounts. **Why:** Spam signal; mutes and blocks; hurts launch day credibility.
- **Don't:** Launch thread same hour as Show HN submit. **Why:** Split founder replies; pick one spike channel per 2h window.
- **Don't:** Auto-DM new followers with pitch. **Why:** Instant unfollow + report risk on X.
- **Don't:** Post only promotional screenshots. **Why:** 8020 rule exists for a reason — dev ICP unfollows brochure accounts.
- **Don't:** Ignore replies for 24h on launch day. **Why:** Algorithm and trust both punish slow founders during spikes.
- **Don't:** Use engagement bait ("like if you agree"). **Why:** B2B dev founders see through it; brand damage is permanent.
- **Don't:** Buy followers or impressions. **Why:** Dead audience; zero signup conversion; violates honest metrics contract.
- **Don't:** Fight critics in public threads. **Why:** One factual reply; dogpiles tank perceived quality.
- **Don't:** Post confidential metrics without consent. **Why:** Build-in-public ≠ leaking customer data.
- **Don't:** Hashtag spam (#SaaS #AI #Startup × 10). **Why:** Looks amateur; no discovery benefit on X in 2026.
- **Don't:** Link to PH/HN with "upvote us" language. **Why:** Cross-platform vote brigading ethics violation.
- **Don't:** Schedule threads without reviewing breaking news. **Why:** Tone-deaf posts during major events get ratio'd.
