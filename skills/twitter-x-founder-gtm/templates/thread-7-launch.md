# 7-tweet launch thread template

**Tweet 1 (hook):** We spent [N] months on [pain]. Today we're launching [Product] — [outcome] for [ICP].

**Tweet 2:** The problem: [specific workflow pain in 2 lines]

**Tweet 3:** What we tried that failed: [honest]

**Tweet 4:** [Screenshot/GIF] — core flow in 15 seconds

**Tweet 5:** Who it's for / not for (honest)

**Tweet 6:** Launch offer or early access note (no fake scarcity)

**Tweet 7:** Try it: [URL]?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=launch_thread

Pin tweet 1. Reply to self with link if needed for algo.
