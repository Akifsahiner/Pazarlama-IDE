# Case study: Engagement pod → ratio + mute wave (lesson)

## Context
B2B SaaS founder joined RT pod before launch. Launch thread asked pod to "boost" + tagged 15 accounts.

## What went wrong
- Pod QRTs with generic "congrats" — ICP detected pattern
- Ratio in replies; two known founders muted
- UTM signups: **4** despite 80k impressions (bot-skewed)

## Fix next cycle
`x_no_engagement_pods`, `x_engagement_before_pitch` 14d, `x_reply_sla_2h`, no mass @

## Outcome (retry)
Second launch: 19k impressions, 28 signups, no pod — smaller reach, real ICP

## Lesson
Dev audience punishes synthetic reach. X skill enforces pods = never.
