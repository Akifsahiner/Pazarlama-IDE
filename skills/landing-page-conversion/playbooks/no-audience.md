# Playbook: Landing Page With No Audience

When you have <100 visitors/day, conversion math is unstable. Optimize for *qualified clicks per visitor* and *insight per visit*, not raw rate.

- **Strip the page to one promise.** Single hero, one screenshot, one CTA. Cut testimonials if you have to fake them — empty space beats fake.
- **Replace social proof with specificity.** No logos? Use a precise outcome statement: "Built by 2 ex-Stripe engineers. Saves the average team 6 hours/week on reconciliation." Specificity is borrowed credibility.
- **Write the headline for ONE persona, by name.** "For Shopify operators running ≥$50k/mo" outperforms "For ecommerce teams." A narrow page beats a broad page when traffic is small.
- **Add a "watch a 90-second walkthrough" link** as the secondary CTA. Cold visitors won't sign up but will watch — and watch-rate is a usable signal at small N.
- **Capture intent, not emails.** Use a "Get notified when X ships" form only if X is concrete. Otherwise use a Calendly link labeled "Talk to the founder (15 min)." Founder calls at low traffic teach you which headline to write next.
- **Run 5-second tests on usertesting.com or PeekUser** with 10 strangers. Ask: "What does this product do, and who is it for?" If 7/10 can't answer, the page is wrong — not your funnel.
- **Don't A/B test yet.** You need ~300 conversions/variant for significance. Instead: ship one strong variant per week and watch qualitative feedback.

KPI ceiling at this stage: ~1.5–3% visitor→signup is plausible; chase clarity, not the rate.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for landing-page-conversion
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
