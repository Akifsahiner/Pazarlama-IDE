# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Use stock illustrations of diverse cartoon people at laptops. **Why:** Signals "template SaaS site"; trust scores in eye-tracking studies are measurably lower than for real product screenshots.
- **Don't:** Put "AI-powered" in the headline. **Why:** Buyers in 2025+ pattern-match this to slop. State the outcome the AI produces, not the technology.
- **Don't:** Hide pricing behind "Contact us" unless ACV is genuinely >$25k. **Why:** Self-serve buyers bounce; you lose data on which plans resonate.
- **Don't:** Show 12 customer logos including 8 nobody recognizes. **Why:** Mixing unknown logos with known ones makes all of them look fake. Show 4–6 strongest.
- **Don't:** Animate the headline letter-by-letter on load. **Why:** Adds 200–600ms before the value prop is readable; first-paint conversions drop.
- **Don't:** Use a carousel for testimonials. **Why:** Carousels average <2% interaction. Show 3 quotes statically.
- **Don't:** Ask for company size, role, and use-case on the signup form. **Why:** Each extra field cuts conversion 7–11%. Ask after signup, in product.
- **Don't:** Stack 6 CTAs on the page. **Why:** The brain treats every CTA as a decision; more decisions = more bounces. One primary, one secondary, max.
- **Don't:** Lead with the company "story." **Why:** Visitors care about their problem, not your founding. Move "About" to footer.
- **Don't:** Use "Submit", "Click here", "Get started" as button text. **Why:** No information scent. Buttons that name the outcome ("Start my free audit") consistently outperform.
- **Don't:** Run an A/B test with <1,000 conversions per arm. **Why:** Underpowered tests produce false wins you'll ship and regret.
- **Don't:** Auto-open a chat widget on first visit. **Why:** Interrupts the read; bounce rates spike. Open only after 30s+ engagement or scroll past 50%.
- **Don't:** Put the demo video behind a form. **Why:** The video is the proof. Gate the *next* step, not the proof itself.
- **Don't:** Use a parallax hero that hides the CTA on scroll. **Why:** Mobile users can't find the button. Conversion craters on iOS Safari.
- **Don't:** Claim "trusted by 10,000+ teams" without a logo bar. **Why:** Unsubstantiated numbers reduce trust more than no numbers.
