# Primary CTA Templates

Match the verb to the visitor's temperature. Cold traffic doesn't sign up; hot traffic resents friction.

---

## Cold traffic (paid ads, SEO, first-touch social)

Primary CTA copy options — pair with a 30-second product loom, not a signup form.

- **See it in 90 seconds** → opens an autoplay-muted video on click
- **Show me how [outcome] works** → e.g. "Show me how 6-hour audits work"
- **Take the 60-second product tour** → interactive walkthrough, no email gate

Secondary CTA: `Read the 5-min explainer →` (link to a single, well-written post)

---

## Warm traffic (email list, retargeting, referral)

Primary CTA copy options — short form, magic-link or Google auth, no credit card.

- **Start free — no card** (the most reliable verb in PLG)
- **Try it on your own data** → uploads a CSV / connects an account
- **Get my [outcome] for free** → e.g. "Get my SOC 2 gap analysis for free"

Secondary CTA: `Book a 15-min walkthrough →` for visitors who hesitate.

---

## Hot traffic (pricing page, comparison page, demo request page)

Primary CTA copy options — schedule directly, don't capture-then-route.

- **Book a 30-min demo** → embed Calendly inline; never bounce to a confirmation page
- **Talk to a founder** (works only if true; insanely effective <Series A)
- **Start a paid trial** → for products with strong activation, route around sales

Secondary CTA: `Get pricing →` (loads on same page; no gate)

## Form field rules

| Field | Keep | Cut |
|---|---|---|
| Work email | ✓ | |
| Company name | ✓ if >$1k ACV | cut otherwise |
| Role / title | ✓ for routing | cut if you don't use it |
| Phone | | cut — kills conversion 20%+ |
| Company size | | move to post-signup survey |
| "How did you hear about us?" | | NEVER on the signup form |

---

## Reject the CTA if it:

- Says "Submit", "Get Started" (vague), "Sign up" (vague), "Click here", "Learn more" as the *primary* action
- Promises something the next page doesn't deliver
- Requires a credit card without saying so
- Has a competing CTA of equal weight in the same viewport
