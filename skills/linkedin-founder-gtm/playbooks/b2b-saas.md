# Playbook: LinkedIn Founder GTM for B2B SaaS

B2B buyers live on LinkedIn. Founder-led GTM here is pipeline work, not brand awareness. Optimize for conversations with decision-makers, not follower count.

- **Day −30: Rewrite founder profile.** Headline formula: `Founder @ [Product] | Helping [ICP] [outcome] | [Proof: "Used by 40 teams" or metric]`. About section: 3 paragraphs — problem you saw, what you built, who it's for. Featured: demo video, case study PDF, booking link.
- **Day −28: Build a 30-day content bank.** 12 post drafts: 6 build/lesson posts, 3 customer/proof posts, 2 carousel frameworks, 1 launch thread outline. Batch in Notion; schedule 3 posts/week.
- **Day −21: Identify 20 ICP "comment targets."** VP/Director-level operators who post weekly in your space. Turn on notifications. Comment substantively (3+ sentences) on 2 posts/day — no pitching.
- **Day −18: Publish first carousel.** Title slide hook: "[ICP pain] costs [specific $ or time]. Here's the 5-step fix." Slides 2–7: framework steps with examples. Slide 8: soft CTA ("We built [Product] for this — link in comments"). Pin comment with URL.
- **Day −14: Customer proof post.** Format: "Before [Customer] / After [Customer]" with named logo, metric, quote. Tag the customer (with permission). Ask them to comment with their experience — customer voice > founder voice for B2B.
- **Day −10: Launch prep — 5-post thread.** Schedule: Day 0 problem story, Day +1 product reveal + demo clip, Day +3 customer metric, Day +5 founder lesson, Day +7 "what's next." Pre-write all five; founder blocks 30 min/day for comment replies.
- **Day −7: Mobilize 5 customers/partners.** Send pre-written LinkedIn post they can paste on launch day: "We've been using [Product] for [timeframe]. [One specific outcome]." Their reach is your distribution.
- **Day −3: DM warm list.** 30 people who've commented on your posts or accepted connection. Message: "Launching [Product] on [date] — would an honest take from someone in [their role] be useful?" Not a pitch; invite to beta or comment.
- **Day 0 (Launch): Problem-story post at 7am local.** 3 paragraphs: pain you lived → what you built → one metric. No "excited to announce." CTA in first comment: demo link + PH link if applicable. Respond to every comment within 30 min for 6 hours.
- **Days +1 to +7: Thread continues.** Customer post, metric post, lesson post per schedule. SDR/AE team briefed: inbound DMs and profile visits will spike; route demo requests same day.
- **Days +8 to +30: ABM-style DM follow-up.** Sales Navigator search: lookalikes of customers who commented. Connection note referencing their recent post. After accept: "Saw you're dealing with [pain from their content] — happy to share how [Customer] solved it."

Realistic outcome: 5–15 qualified demos in launch month, 800–3,000 new followers (60%+ ICP), 20–50 DM conversations, 3–8 closed deals attributable within 90 days.
