# Playbook: LinkedIn Founder GTM With an Existing Email List

The list seeds LinkedIn proof; LinkedIn feeds the list. Run both on the same narrative spine — never two different stories.

---

## Day −21 to −14: Profile + content foundation

- **Day −21:** Rewrite headline and About using `templates/headline-formula.md`. Pin waitlist/demo link in Featured with UTM `linkedin_profile`.
- **Day −20:** Email list (power segment only): "I'm posting publicly about [problem domain] for the next few weeks — here's why." Link to founder profile, not company page.
- **Day −19 to −14:** Publish 3 posts from Week 1 of `templates/post-grid-14d.md` before asking the list to engage. You need content live before driving traffic.
- **Daily:** 30-min comment block on ICP leader posts. Track profile visits baseline.

---

## Day −14: Cross-pollinate list → LinkedIn

- **Email subject:** I wrote this for you first — now it's on LinkedIn
- **Body:** Link to your best-performing post (teach post, not sell). Ask: "If it resonates, a comment with your take helps more than a like — I reply to every one."
- **Goal:** 10–30 substantive comments from list members; screenshot-worthy thread for Day −10 post.
- **Do not** ask the list to "boost" via engagement pods — LinkedIn detects unnatural spikes.

---

## Day −10: Proof post fueled by list

- **LinkedIn post (Template P from post grid):** Customer story or beta quote — ideally from a list subscriber who opted in.
- **Email (same day, 4h later):** "This went live on LinkedIn today — [customer] shared how they [outcome]." Link to post. Non-signups see social proof; signups feel seen.
- **Tag** list members who commented on prior posts as `linkedin-advocate` for personal thank-you DMs.

---

## Day −7: List as comment army (ethical version)

- **Email to engaged segment:** "Launching a thread on [day] about [topic]. No obligation — but if you've used [product / read the posts], your honest comment on the thread would mean a lot."
- **Send the draft post** 24h early so comments are informed, not generic "great post!"
- **Launch post:** Origin story or contrarian take — not product pitch. See post grid D12.
- **Founder replies** to every comment within 30 min for first 3 hours.

---

## Day −3 to −1: DM funnel from dual channels

- **LinkedIn:** End launch-week posts with DM keyword ("early," "demo," "waitlist").
- **Email:** "If LinkedIn isn't your thing — reply here and I'll send the link directly."
- **Process DMs** using `templates/dm-script.md` Template 3 for list-adjacent warm leads.
- **Track:** DM source (LinkedIn organic vs email referral) in a simple spreadsheet or CRM.

---

## Day 0: Launch coordination

- **6am:** LinkedIn launch thread post (5-post sequence over 5 days — see principles).
- **7am:** Email power segment with link to LinkedIn post + direct product/waitlist link.
- **11am:** Email general list — different subject, same links.
- **All day:** Reply on LinkedIn AND email; don't pick one channel.
- **Evening post:** "What we learned from today's launch" — real metric, not hype.

---

## Day +1 to +7: Compound earned reach

- **Day +1:** Email thank-you with LinkedIn screenshot (impressions, meaningful comments, not vanity likes).
- **Day +3:** LinkedIn post = customer quote + email excerpt (with permission).
- **Day +5:** LinkedIn carousel repurposed from best-performing email content.
- **Day +7:** Email recap: "Top 3 posts this week" with links — re-engages cold list segment.

---

## Segment-specific tactics

| Segment | LinkedIn tactic | Email tactic |
|---------|-----------------|--------------|
| Power users | Personal DM before big posts | Early draft preview |
| Interested, low LinkedIn | Reply-to-get-link | Full post text in email body |
| Cold list | Retarget via LinkedIn ads on email list upload (if budget) | One send only — launch day |
| Unsubscribed | Organic only | Do not re-email |

## Realistic outcomes (5–20k engaged list)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| List → LinkedIn post comment | 1–3% of engaged segment |
| Profile views (launch week) | 3–10× baseline |
| DM conversations started | 15–40 in 14 days |
| Pipeline attributed to LinkedIn | 2–5 qualified conversations |

---

## Anti-patterns

- Company page cross-posts instead of founder voice — 5–10× reach penalty
- Email blasts asking for likes on every post — trains list to ignore you
- Identical copy on LinkedIn and email — write natively for each channel
- Posting launch thread before 3 weeks of consistent content — no audience trust yet

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for linkedin-founder-gtm
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
