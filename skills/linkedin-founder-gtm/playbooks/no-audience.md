# Playbook: LinkedIn Founder GTM With No Audience

Zero followers, zero connections in your ICP. LinkedIn GTM from cold starts with comments and connection strategy — posting alone into the void won't work for 60 days.

- **Days 1–3: Profile setup.** Headline: `[Building X] | [ICP outcome] | Documenting the journey`. About: why you're building, who it's for, invite to follow along. No product pitch in About — build trust first. Connect with 50 people: former colleagues, investors, 20 ICP matches (personalized note: "Saw your work at [Company] — building in [space], would love to connect").
- **Days 4–10: Comment-first strategy.** Zero posts. Spend 45 min/day commenting on 5–8 posts from ICP leaders, peer founders, and industry voices. Substantive comments only (insight, question, or shared experience). Goal: 10 profile visits/day, 3–5 connection accepts from ICP.
- **Days 11–14: First post — build log, not launch.** "Week 2 of building [Product]. Here's what we learned about [ICP pain] from 5 customer interviews." Include one specific quote or data point. No product link. Goal: 200–500 impressions, 5+ comments.
- **Days 15–21: Post 2×/week + continue commenting.** Alternate: build update (screenshot + lesson) and "problem post" (contrarian take on ICP pain). Reply to every comment within 2 hours. Target: 500 followers, 15 DMs from engaged commenters.
- **Days 22–25: Recruit 10 design partners via DM.** Message people who've commented: "You clearly care about [topic] — we're looking for 10 [ICP role]s to shape [Product]. Free access + direct line to founder. Interested?" Convert commenters to users before launch.
- **Days 26–28: First carousel.** Hook: "Most [ICP role]s get [pain] wrong. Here's the framework we use." 8 slides, no product mention until slide 8 soft CTA. Export 1080×1350. Post Tuesday or Wednesday 8am local.
- **Day 29: Soft launch post.** "After 30 days building in public, we're opening [Product] to 50 beta users." Link in first comment. Ask design partners to comment with honest experience — social proof from peers, not founder.
- **Days 30–45: Sustain 3 posts/week.** Mix: build logs, customer quotes (from design partners), one framework carousel/week. Comment on 5 posts/day. Track: profile visits, DM starts, demo requests.
- **Days 46–60: Launch thread when ready.** Only after 1,000+ followers or 30+ design partners with proof. 5-post thread over 7 days. By then, comment history and connections give posts initial velocity.

Realistic outcome: 800–1,500 followers in 60 days, 40–60% ICP ratio, 10–25 DM conversations, 8–15 design partners, 5–12 demos from LinkedIn alone.

4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast
