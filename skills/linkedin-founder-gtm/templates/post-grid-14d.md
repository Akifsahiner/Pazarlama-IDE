# 14-Day LinkedIn Post Grid — Template

Two weeks to establish cadence before a launch or waitlist push. Mix: 70% teach, 20% proof, 10% sell. Post Tue–Thu primary; Mon/Fri optional. Best times: 7–9am or 12pm in your ICP's primary timezone.

**Format key:** T = text | C = carousel (8–10 slides) | V = video/native doc | P = proof/customer story

---

## Week 1 — Establish the problem space

| Day | Format | Hook (first line) | Body arc | CTA |
|-----|--------|-------------------|----------|-----|
| **D1 Tue** | T | Most [ICP plural] lose [metric] because of [non-obvious cause]. | 3 paragraphs: mistake → why it persists → one actionable fix | "What's your version of this?" |
| **D2 Wed** | C | The [framework name] we use to [outcome] in [timeframe] | Slides: hook → problem → 4 steps → example → proof slide → CTA | "Save this. DM me [keyword] for the template." |
| **D3 Thu** | T | I [embarrassing mistake / loss] so you don't have to. | Build log: what happened, what we changed, metric delta | "Follow for weekly build updates." |
| **D4 Fri** | T | 3 signs [ICP] is ready for [solution category]: | Numbered list, 1–2 sentences each, specific not generic | Poll or question in comments |
| **D5 Sat/Sun** | — | Rest or comment-only (30 min on ICP leader posts) | — | — |

---

## Week 2 — Proof + soft sell ramp

| Day | Format | Hook (first line) | Body arc | CTA |
|-----|--------|-------------------|----------|-----|
| **D8 Mon** | P | [Customer name] cut [metric] from [X] to [Y] in [timeframe]. | Story: before state → what they tried → what changed → quote | "Full case study in comments." |
| **D9 Tue** | T | We surveyed [N] [ICP plural]. One result surprised us: | Share 1 data point; tease product angle without naming until last line | "Agree or disagree?" |
| **D10 Wed** | C | How [ICP role] should evaluate [tool category] in 2025 | Comparison framework — not a vendor hit piece; include "when NOT to buy" slide | "Link to checklist in Featured." |
| **D11 Thu** | V | [15-sec screen demo or talking head] — watch the first 3 seconds | Hook on video: outcome on screen; caption repeats hook + context | "Waitlist / demo link in comments." |
| **D12 Fri** | T | Launching [product] in [N] days. Here's why we built it. | Origin story in prose — no bullet feature list | "DM 'early' if you want founding access." |
| **D13 Sat** | — | Comment sprint: 10 substantive comments on ICP posts | — | — |
| **D14 Sun** | T | 14 days of posting. What worked (numbers): | Recap: top post impressions, 3 ICP comments received, 1 lesson | "What should I write about next week?" |

---

## Post skeleton — text post (copy structure)

```
[Hook — contrarian claim, number, or open loop. Must work standalone before "see more."]

[Context — 2–3 short paragraphs. One idea per paragraph. Line breaks every 1–2 sentences for mobile.]

[Proof — specific number, customer, or screenshot reference]

[CTA — question, DM keyword, or "link in comments" not "link in bio"]

—
[Optional P.S. with secondary CTA or teaser for next post]
```

---

## Carousel skeleton — 8 slides

1. **Hook slide:** Bold claim + subtitle (1080×1350)
2. **Problem:** What [ICP] gets wrong today
3. **Step 1:** Actionable, named
4. **Step 2**
5. **Step 3**
6. **Step 4** (optional — 3 steps is fine)
7. **Proof:** Metric, logo strip, or quote
8. **CTA:** Follow + DM keyword + one benefit

Export PDF from Figma/Canva; upload as document post.

---

## Engagement block (daily, 30 min — non-negotiable)

- [ ] Comment substantively (3+ sentences) on 5 posts from ICP leaders
- [ ] Reply to every comment on your post within 2 hours of posting
- [ ] Send 2 DMs using `dm-script.md` Template 1 to people who engaged

---

## Metrics to track per post

| Metric | Target (early founder) | Action if below |
|--------|------------------------|-----------------|
| Impressions | 500+ by Day 2 | Test hook rewrite repost in 7 days |
| ICP comments | 2+ from target titles | Comment more; post less sell |
| Profile visits | 20+ per post | Stronger hook or carousel format |
| DM conversations | 1 per week | End posts with DM keyword CTA |

---

## Reject the grid if it:

- Has product mentions in more than 2 of 14 posts (overselling)
- Posts daily without engagement block (reach collapses)
- Reuses identical hooks across consecutive days
- Schedules posts without founder available to reply same day
