# LinkedIn Headline Formulas — Templates

220 characters visible in feed previews. Lead with outcome for the ICP, not your job title. No buzzwords — specific numbers and verbs.

---

## Formula (default)

```
[Role] @ [Company] — helping [ICP] [outcome verb] [metric/timeframe] | [Proof point] | [CTA]
```

**Example:**
```
Founder @ Acme — helping ops teams cut onboarding from 14 days to 2 | 40+ SaaS teams in beta | DM "demo"
```

---

## Template 1 — Outcome-first (best for founders with traction)

```
Building [product] for [ICP] who need [outcome] without [pain] | [N customers / metric] | [CTA: DM "X" / link in featured]
```

**Filled example:**
```
Building Linear for marketing ops — ship campaigns without the spreadsheet chaos | 40 teams, 2M tasks shipped | DM "waitlist"
```

**Checklist:**
- [ ] ICP is a role, not "businesses" or "everyone"
- [ ] Proof is a number or named customer tier
- [ ] CTA is one action (DM keyword beats "link in bio" on LinkedIn)

---

## Template 2 — Authority + niche (best for consultants / serial founders)

```
[Years] in [domain]. Now [current mission] for [ICP]. [Proof: exits, scale, prior company]. [CTA].
```

**Filled example:**
```
10 years in B2B SaaS. Now fixing revenue ops for Series A teams. Ex-Stripe, 2 exits. Weekly GTM breakdowns — follow along.
```

---

## Template 3 — Contrarian hook (best for cold-start profiles under 2k followers)

```
Most [ICP plural] [common mistake]. I [what you do differently]. [Product/Newsletter/Community] → [outcome]. [CTA].
```

**Filled example:**
```
Most founders post on LinkedIn like brands. I document building in public — wins and failures. 0 → 500 ICP followers in 90 days. Follow for the playbook.
```

---

## Template 4 — Pre-launch / waitlist (best during GTM ramp)

```
Launching [product] [month] — [outcome] for [ICP] | [N] on waitlist | DM "early" for founding access
```

**Filled example:**
```
Launching Atlas in March — automated compliance for fintech ops teams | 2,400 on waitlist | DM "early" for founding access
```

## About section — first 3 lines (visible before "see more")

Use the same hook discipline as posts. Pattern:

```
Line 1: [Contrarian claim or specific pain in 12 words]
Line 2: [What you built / do + mechanism]
Line 3: [Proof + who it's for]

[Blank line]

If you're [ICP role] struggling with [pain], I write about [topics] and build [product].

→ [CTA: DM me "X" | Featured link | Newsletter]
```

**Example:**
```
Most onboarding playbooks assume you have a training team. You don't.

I built Acme so ops leads at 20–200 person SaaS companies can onboard reps in 2 days, not 2 weeks — without hiring an enablement manager.

40 teams in beta. $2.1M ARR onboarded through the product last quarter.

If you're a Head of Ops or RevOps lead, I post weekly frameworks from the trenches.

→ DM "demo" for a 15-min walkthrough
```

## Featured section — pin these 3 items

1. **Waitlist or demo link** — UTM tagged `linkedin_profile`
2. **Best-performing post or carousel** — proof of expertise
3. **Customer story or case study** — PDF or link

---

## Reject the headline if it:

- Starts with "Passionate about" or "Helping companies leverage"
- Lists 4+ credentials separated by pipes (reads like a résumé)
- Has no ICP and no outcome — "CEO | Investor | Speaker" tells buyers nothing
- Uses "thought leader," "visionary," or "disrupting"
- CTA asks people to "join my journey"

## Ethical notes

- Don't claim customer counts or metrics you can't substantiate
- "Ex-[BigCo]" is fine; implying endorsement from that employer is not
- DM CTAs should be honored within 24 hours — ghosting destroys the funnel
