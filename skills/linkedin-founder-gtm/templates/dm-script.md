# LinkedIn DM Scripts — Templates

DM outbound works when it's research, not pitch. Never paste the same message to 50 people. Reference something specific they posted, wrote, or built. 3–5 sentences max.

---

## Template 1 — Post-triggered outreach (default; highest reply rate)

**When:** They commented on your post, liked 3+ of your posts, or published on a topic you solve.

```
Hey [first name] — your comment on [topic] resonated. Especially the bit about [specific detail from their comment/post].

We're working on [related problem] with [product] — early, but [one proof point]. Not pitching; genuinely curious how you're handling [specific sub-problem] at [company].

Would a 10-min compare-notes call be useful? No deck, just conversation.

— [your first name]
```

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Reference is from the last 14 days
- [ ] No link in first message — link comes after they reply
- [ ] "Not pitching" is true — lead with curiosity

---

## Template 2 — ICP cold outreach (use after 30 days of consistent posting)

**When:** Profile matches ICP, no prior engagement. Requires 1 line of genuine research.

```
Hey [first name] — saw [company]'s [recent launch / hiring post / product update]. [One specific observation that shows you looked].

I build [product] for [ICP role plural] dealing with [pain]. [Customer name or metric] saw [outcome] in [timeframe].

If [pain] is on your radar this quarter, happy to share what's working for similar teams — 15 min, your call if it's relevant.

— [your first name]
```

---

## Template 3 — Waitlist / launch invite (warm only)

**When:** They've engaged with 2+ posts OR are on your email list OR met at an event.

```
Hey [first name] — we're opening [product] to a small batch next [week/month]. You're on the short list because [specific reason: engaged on X post / beta feedback / intro from Y].

It's [one-line outcome]. Founding access is free for [trial period] — looking for 5 [ICP role plural] who'll give blunt feedback.

Interested? I'll send the link — no pressure if timing's off.

— [your first name]
```

---

## Template 4 — Follow-up (send once, 5–7 days after no reply)

```
Hey [first name] — bumping this in case it got buried. Totally fine if it's not a fit or bad timing.

If useful later, I'm here. Either way, appreciated your [post/article] on [topic].

— [your first name]
```

**Never send a third message without a new trigger** (they posted again, mutual connection intro, etc.).

---

## Template 5 — Post-call follow-up

```
Great talking today, [first name]. As discussed:

→ [One specific thing you promised: doc, intro, trial link]
→ [Their stated priority] — I'll follow up [specific date] to see how [specific metric] looks

Link: [URL with UTM linkedin_dm]

Ping me anytime before then.

— [your first name]
```

---

## Reply handling — when they say yes

```
Awesome — here's a Calendly for 15 min: [link]

Or if easier, throw me 2 times that work and I'll send an invite.

Looking forward to it.
```

## Reply handling — when they say "send info"

```
Here's a 90-sec walkthrough: [Loom link]

And the one-pager if you want to share internally: [Notion/PDF link]

Happy to jump on a call if any of it sparks questions.
```

---

## Reject the DM if it:

- Opens with "I hope this finds you well" or "I'm reaching out because"
- Contains a pitch deck link in message 1
- Is identical to 10+ other messages sent that day (LinkedIn flags spam)
- Name-drops a mutual connection who didn't actually intro you
- Uses fake "I noticed you're hiring" when they're not

## Ethical notes

- LinkedIn ToS restricts automation and bulk outreach — hand-send or use tools within limits
- If they say no or don't reply twice, stop — persistence reads as harassment
- Don't scrape emails from profiles to dual-channel spam
- Disclose if you're fundraising and the "call" is partly investor pitch
