# Playbook: Aggressive ICP Precision Research

Evidence URL mandatory. No invented titles.

## Preconditions
- [ ] ICP hypothesis written
- [ ] Allowed sources (LinkedIn, sites, directories)
- [ ] Scorecard template ready

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Vague ICP | Desk research only |
| standard | Clear ICP | 25 scored leads |
| aggressive | Outbound ready | 50+ with evidence URLs |

## Timeline
- **T-7**: Source matrix
- **T-5…T-1**: Score leads
- **H0**: Hand off top 20 to outreach

## Tactic stack
1. **`lead_evidence_scorecard`** — Fit 1–5 + evidence URL per row
2. Disqualify without public proof
3. Tag triggers (funding, hiring, stack)

## Orchestration
- Feeds `outreach-drafting` tier-3 personalization

## Realistic outcomes
- Precision > volume; 20 great > 200 thin

## Kill / pivot rules
- <40% of list has evidence → stop scraping; refine ICP

## Ethics line
- No scraped personal emails from ToS-violating sources; GDPR/CCPA respect
