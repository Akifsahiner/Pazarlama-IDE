# Playbook: Lead Research With an Existing Email List

Your list is a research asset, not just a blast channel. Mine it for lookalike accounts and upgrade subscribers into qualified outbound targets.

- **Segment subscribers into research tiers first.** Tier 1: work email + ICP industry + engaged (opened 3+ emails). Tier 2: work email, low engagement. Tier 3: personal email — research only if role signals match. Never bulk-outreach personal emails without explicit opt-in.
- **Extract lookalike companies from Tier 1 domains.** If 15 subscribers share `@acme.com`-style domains across 8 companies, those 8 companies are proven ICP — research 2–3 additional contacts at each (not just the subscriber).
- **Build lookalike list from subscriber firmographics.** Group by: industry, company size band, role title (from signup form or LinkedIn lookup). Top cluster = primary lookalike profile. Search 10–15 net-new accounts matching that cluster.
- **Use engagement data as why-now signal.** Clicked "pricing" email → budget consideration active. Clicked "integration" content → technical evaluation. Replied to any email → Tier A for founder personal outreach, not automated sequence.
- **Cross-reference unsubscribes and "not a fit" replies.** "We're B2C" or "Too small for this" refines negative ICP — document exclusion criteria to sharpen future searches.
- **Dedupe list members from outbound pack.** Subscribers already in funnel get nurture, not cold outreach. Lead research output should flag `already_subscriber: true` to prevent double-touch in `outreach-drafting`.
- **Expand with "companies like [top subscriber company]."** Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator lookalike, Crunchbase similar, or manual search: same stage, same stack, same hiring patterns. 10 net-new accounts per top-5 subscriber company.
- **Deliver hybrid pack: 10 list-derived + 10 net-new.** List-derived leads have highest conversion probability. Net-new proves the ICP extends beyond current audience.
- **Respect permission boundaries.** List research uses data you already have; net-new research uses public profiles only. Never sell or share subscriber emails in research output.

Track: lookalike match rate (% net-new accounts matching subscriber cluster), subscriber→outbound overlap (should be 0 duplicate touches), A-tier rate on net-new vs list-derived.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for lead-research
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
