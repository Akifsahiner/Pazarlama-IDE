# Playbook: Lead Research for B2B SaaS (LinkedIn + Product Hunt)

B2B SaaS outbound starts with high-intent public signals — job changes, stack posts, PH launches, and competitor engagement. Target 20 A/B-tier accounts in 2–3 hours of focused research.

- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator search (primary source).** Filters: job title (ICP role), company headcount (stage band), industry, geography. Keywords in profile: competitor names, complementary tools, pain phrases from product profile. Save search; export top 30; score down to 20.
- **PH sourcing for early-adopter champions.** Search PH launches in adjacent categories (last 90 days). Makers and hunters who upvoted competitors = proven category interest. Note: PH profile → LinkedIn cross-reference for B2B role verification.
- **"Commenting on competitor content" mining.** Find 10 ICP posts that mention competitor pain in last 30 days. Author + commenters with substantive takes = warm-intent leads. Capture post URL as why-now evidence.
- **Job-change triggers (highest reply rate).** LinkedIn alert: ICP title, joined new company in last 90 days. New role = budget for new tools + mandate to fix inherited mess. Note start date and previous company.
- **Stack-change signals from engineering blogs and job posts.** "We're migrating from [incumbent]" in eng blog, or job req listing your integration partners = implementation window open.
- **Score every candidate on lead scorecard.** Must-haves: right role, right stage, relevant stack, identifiable pain, why-now ≤30 days. A = 5/5 must-haves + why-now. B = 4/5. C = exclude.
- **Find 2 contacts per A-tier account when possible.** Champion (ICP role) + technical evaluator (if product has API/integration) or economic buyer (VP+ for deals >$5k ACV). Same company, different outreach angles later.
- **Never invent emails.** Deliver: name, title, company, LinkedIn URL, why-now, source URL, tier. Email discovery is founder action (Hunter.io, contact page, or InMail).
- **Source matrix mandatory.** Tag each lead: `linkedin_search`, `ph_maker`, `ph_hunter`, `competitor_engagement`, `job_change`. Dedupe by person (not just domain).

Track: icp_fit_rate (≥50% A-tier), why-now freshness (% with trigger <30 days), source mix (≥2 channels represented).

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for lead-research
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
