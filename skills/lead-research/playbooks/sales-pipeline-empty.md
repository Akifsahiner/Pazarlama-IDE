# Playbook: Sales Pipeline Empty

ICP precision before volume. Evidence URLs mandatory; tier-3 personalization only for top accounts.

## Preconditions
- [ ] ICP hypothesis written (role, company size, trigger)
- [ ] No existing qualified pipeline (`sales_pipeline_empty: true`)
- [ ] Allowed research sources (public LinkedIn, company sites)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Vague ICP | Desk research, 10 scored leads |
| standard | Clear ICP | 25 leads with evidence URLs |
| aggressive | Outbound-ready | 50+ scorecard + tier-3 for top 20 |

## Timeline
- **T-7**: Source matrix + disqualifiers
- **T-5…T-1**: Score leads (`lead_evidence_scorecard`)
- **H0**: Hand off top 20 to outreach with tier tags
- **D+7**: Measure reply rate; refine ICP

## Tactic stack
1. **`lead_evidence_scorecard`** — Fit 1–5 + evidence URL per row
2. Tag triggers: funding, hiring, stack change
3. Disqualify without public proof
4. Export only tier 2+ to outreach

## Orchestration
- Research primary; outreach-drafting receives scored list only
- No spray — volume follows evidence quality

## Realistic outcomes
- 20 great leads > 200 thin rows
- Reply rate benchmark set before first send (e.g. ≥8% tier 2)

## Kill / pivot rules
- <40% rows have evidence URL → stop scraping; refine ICP
- Reply <2% after 40 sends → rewrite opener; pause volume

## Ethics line
- No scraped personal emails from ToS-violating sources; GDPR/CCPA respect
