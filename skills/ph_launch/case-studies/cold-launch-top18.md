# Case study: Cold launch → Top 18 after 21-day runway (anonymous)

## Context
Indie tool, 0 email list, ~80 Twitter followers. Founder wanted “#1 on PH.” Initial instinct: launch next week.

## Tactic stack applied
1. Honest ceiling conversation: Top 1 unrealistic → aim Top 20 after runway
2. `ph_citizen_mode_14d` + recruit 30 design partners outside PH
3. Upcoming page + 12 personal comment commitments (not 100)
4. Self-hunt Tuesday 12:01am PT; no mass “upvote” asks
5. D+1 teardown post drove more signups than launch day

## Outcome
- Day rank: **#18**
- ~1.1k visits, ~90 signups
- ~200 new followers from teardown narrative
- Rank alone did not move revenue; teardown + waitlist referral did

## Lesson
Do not run `aggressive-top-1` cold. Spend 21–30 days on `no-audience` runway. Aggressive without assets wastes the one PH shot and teaches the wrong lesson (“PH doesn’t work”).
