# Case study: SaaS with ~8k engaged list → Top 3 (anonymous)

## Context
B2B SaaS for ops teams. Stage: early growth. Assets: ~8k email list (engaged open rate ~32%), 40 design partners, real product screenshots, founder active on LinkedIn. Goal: PH day Top 5 for awareness + inbound demos.

## Tactic stack applied
1. `ph_citizen_mode_14d` — two weeks of real PH comments in category
2. `ph_upcoming_page_7d` — ~420 notify subscribers
3. `ph_comment_squad_10` — 18 personal yeses for first-hour questions
4. `ph_gallery_4_slides` — workflow GIF + “14h → 19min response” proof slide
5. `ph_submit_1201_pt` — Wednesday 12:01am PT self-hunt
6. Three email waves at H+6 / H+9 / H+12 + LinkedIn founder post at H+9
7. `ph_comment_reply_sla_30m` — founder blocked calendar

## Outcome
- Day rank: **#3**
- ~12k PH visits, ~2.1k signups in 48h (mix of curiosity + ICP)
- ~22 demo requests in 14 days attributable via UTM
- No ToS flags; comment quality praised in teardown thread

## Lesson
Warm list + comment squad + hour-by-hour replies beat “famous hunter.” Top 1 still not guaranteed — Top 3 was the honest aggressive ceiling for this asset base.
