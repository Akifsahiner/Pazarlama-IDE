# Email wave subject lines (PH launch day)

Sender: founder name. Single CTA: Product Hunt URL. Never “please upvote.”

## Wave 1 — H+6 (engaged / opened 3 of last 5)
- `{{product_name}} is live on Product Hunt — would love your honest take`
- `{{outcome_phrase}} — we just shipped on PH`
- `You're on the early list: {{product_name}} launched`

## Wave 2 — H+9 (general list)
- `{{product_name}}: {{tagline_under_60}}`
- `We launched something for {{icp_persona}}`
- `Live now: {{one_benefit}}`

## Wave 3 — H+12 (cold / missed wave 1–2)
- `In case you missed it — {{product_name}} on PH`
- `Quick link: {{product_name}} + what we learned so far`

## Body rules
- Ask for **honest comment / feedback**, not votes
- One link
- PH-only deal (if any) in P.S. with clear expiry
