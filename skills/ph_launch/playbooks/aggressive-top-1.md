# Playbook: PH Aggressive Top-1 War Room

Ethical maximum intensity for Product Hunt day rank. Use only when warm distribution (engaged email list ≥5k *or* ≥100 personal supporters who will *comment*) is real — not aspirational.

## Preconditions
- [ ] ICP is active on PH (indie / early SaaS / prosumer) — else skip PH
- [ ] Engaged email list ≥5,000 **or** documented 100+ comment-capable supporters
- [ ] Tagline ≤60 chars naming the *outcome*, not the tech
- [ ] Gallery: ≥4 slides with real product UI (no mockups-as-product)
- [ ] Tracking: UTM `utm_source=producthunt` + PH-day deal link ready
- [ ] Founder calendar blocked launch day (Pacific window)
- [ ] Hunter strategy decided (self-hunt default; paid hunter = never)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Cold / <100 supporters | Postpone 21–30 days; citizen mode first (`no-audience`) |
| standard | Warm list 1–5k or strong community | Staggered email + honest comments (`with-email-list`) |
| aggressive | ≥5k engaged list **and** 10 comment squad locked | This playbook — hour-by-hour war room |

**Honest ceiling:** Without a warm list or personal comment squad, Top 1 is unrealistic. Expect Top 15–25 cold. Do not invent supporters.

## Timeline

| Phase | When | Action |

## Tactic stack

1. **`ph_citizen_mode_14d` (T-30)** — 3 upvotes + 1 substantive comment/day for 14 days in-category. Metric: 14 days logged.
2. **`ph_hunter_dm_14d` (T-21)** — Shortlist 5 hunters with recent category wins; DM 14 days out; never pay. Metric: 1 yes or self-hunt locked.
3. **`ph_upcoming_page_7d` (T-14)** — Upcoming + notify funnel; LP primary CTA = upcoming. Metric: ≥200 notify subscribers when possible.
4. **`ph_comment_squad_10` (T-10)** — 10 people commit to first-hour *honest comments* (not “upvote us”). Metric: 10 confirmations in a sheet.
5. **`ph_gallery_4_slides` (T-7)** — Slide1 hero+tagline, Slide2 workflow GIF, Slide3 proof metric, Slide4 PH-only deal. Metric: 4 real-UI slides shipped.
6. **`ph_maker_comment_story` (T-3)** — First-person story: problem → build → ask for feedback. Metric: draft approved, <400 words.
7. **`ph_submit_1201_pt` (H0)** — Submit Tue/Wed 12:01am PT only. Metric: submission timestamp logged.
8. **`ph_email_wave_1_6am` (H+6)** — Wave 1 to opened 3/5 segment; founder from-name; single CTA = PH URL. Metric: send logged.
9. **`ph_email_wave_2_9am` (H+9)** — Wave 2 general + parallel LinkedIn founder post. Metric: both live.
10. **`ph_email_wave_3_12pm` (H+12)** — Wave 3 cold reframe. Metric: send logged.
11. **`ph_comment_reply_sla_30m` (H0–H+18)** — Every comment answered ≤30 min; non-English via DeepL OK. Metric: zero unreplied >30m in first 6h.
12. **`ph_teardown_post_d1` (D+1)** — Public teardown (rank, visits, lessons) + thank-you email. Metric: post + email published.

## Orchestration

- **Primary:** Product Hunt page (gallery, maker comment, comment threads)
- **Parallel velocity:** ESP (3 waves) + founder LinkedIn + personal DMs to comment squad
- **Do not:** Tweet “upvote us,” Discord spam, or buy traffic to PH URL before H0
- **Sales/B2B:** Brief inbound for demo spike; PH deal link attributed separately

## Realistic outcomes

| Profile | Rank band | Visits | Signups | Notes |
|---------|-----------|--------|---------|-------|
| Aggressive (≥5–20k engaged list) | #1–5 | 5k–25k | 1k–4k | Often PH newsletter |
| Standard warm (1–5k) | #5–15 | 3k–10k | 400–1.5k | Solid awareness |
| Cold / no list | #15–25 | 0.8k–1.5k | 60–200 | Use `no-audience` runway instead |

Attribution window: 7–60 days for pipeline; day rank ≠ revenue.

## Kill / pivot rules

- T-14: if <50 warm supporters confirmed → **downgrade to standard** or postpone 21 days
- H+3: if <10 upvotes *and* 0 substantive comments → check listing visibility; do **not** beg for votes
- H+12: if engagement dead → stop email waves; pivot to teardown narrative + LinkedIn lesson post
- Post-launch: if signups quality low (ICP mismatch) → kill PH as primary channel next cycle

## Ethics line

- **Never:** paid hunters, bot traffic, “please upvote” DMs, early URL spam that looks manufactured, or any ToS-breaking engagement rings
- **Always:** ask for honest comments / feedback; disclose PH-only deals clearly
- Aggressive means intensity of *legitimate* orchestration — not ToS violations
