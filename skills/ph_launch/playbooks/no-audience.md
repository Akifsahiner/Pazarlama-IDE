# Playbook: PH Launch With No Audience

Most "I launched and got 12 upvotes" stories come from launching cold. Don't. Build the runway first, then schedule the launch.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Accept honest ceiling: Top 1 is unrealistic cold
- [ ] Commit 21–30 days before scheduling PH
- [ ] Willing to do PH citizen mode (real comments)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Brand new to PH | Citizen mode only; no date yet |
| standard | After 100-supporter pool | Schedule Tue/Wed 12:01am PT |
| aggressive | Do **not** use aggressive-top-1 cold | Upgrade only after warm assets |

## Timeline
- **T-30→T-16**: Citizen mode — upvote 3/day, 1 substantive comment, follow 20 makers
- **T-21**: Recruit 30 design partners outside PH; get 5 quotes
- **T-14**: Hunter DM (no pay) or lock self-hunt
- **T-7**: Upcoming page; notify list
- **T-1**: 10 people promise first-hour *comments*
- **H0**: 12:01am PT Tue/Wed submit; sleep; wake 5am PT
- **D+1**: Teardown post

## Tactic stack
1. Don't launch in week 1 — build 100-supporter pool first
2. `ph_citizen_mode_14d` — contribution patterns matter for curation
3. Recruit 30 design partners (X, IH, Slack, cold ICP email)
4. Hunter DM 14 days out — never pay
5. Upcoming page 7 days out (even 200 notifies matter)
6. Night-before: 10 comment commitments (not upvotes)
7. `ph_submit_1201_pt` + reply until 8pm PT
8. `ph_teardown_post_d1` — often bigger than the PH page itself

## Orchestration
- Outside-PH recruitment first; PH is the flash, not the whole campaign
- Parallel: IndieHackers build log, founder LinkedIn

## Realistic outcomes
Cold: ranks **#15–25**, 800–1,500 visits, 60–200 signups, ~30 net new followers.

## Kill / pivot rules
- If at T-14 you still have <30 reachable humans → postpone another 14 days
- If launch day is dead by H+6 → stop begging; write teardown and invest in waitlist

## Ethics line
- Never ask for upvotes; ask for honest comments
- No paid hunters, farms, or bots
