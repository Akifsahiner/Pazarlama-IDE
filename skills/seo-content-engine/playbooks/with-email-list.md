# Playbook: With Email List (SEO + Nurture Loop)

You have ≥1k emails and some indexable content. SEO pages capture cold traffic; email captures warm repeat visits. **Do not** duplicate waitlist referral mechanics here — this is organic + newsletter compounding.

- **T-14: GSC baseline** — segment organic vs email-driven signups in analytics.
- **T-10: Ship alternatives page** — email drives first 200 visits for engagement signal (not paid traffic).
- **T-7: Newsletter excerpt** — "We published an honest [Competitor] vs [You] breakdown" to engaged segment only.
- **T-3: Internal links** from top 3 blog posts to new commercial page.
- **H0: Index request** on hub URL after email spike proves engagement.

Pair with `email-nurture-sequence` for launch week — SEO is always-on, not day spike.

## Preconditions
- [ ] `email_list_size` ≥1,000 or active waitlist with double opt-in
- [ ] ≥2 indexable routes
- [ ] ESP can tag "engaged" segment (opened 2 of last 5)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | List <2k | SEO pillar + monthly newsletter link |
| standard | 2k–10k engaged | Cluster + 1 email push to engaged |
| aggressive | ≥10k + docs live | Full programmatic cluster + email excerpt wave |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Cluster map + GSC baseline
- **T-10**: Alternatives + vs #1 live
- **T-7**: Engaged email excerpt (single CTA = new page)
- **T-3**: Metadata + schema pass
- **D+30**: Refresh low-CTR pages

## Tactic stack

## Orchestration
- SEO pages = durable assets; email = distribution burst to engaged only
- Never buy traffic to inflate GSC CTR

## Realistic outcomes
- Email excerpt: 8–25% CTR to content page on engaged segment
- Organic compounding: 2–4× sessions by D+90 vs no cluster

## Kill / pivot rules
- Email blast to full list for SEO page → unsubscribe spike; engaged only
- Page bounce >75% from email → fix title/message match

## Ethics line
- No "we're #1 on Google" in email without GSC screenshot
- Honest comparison in email matches page content
