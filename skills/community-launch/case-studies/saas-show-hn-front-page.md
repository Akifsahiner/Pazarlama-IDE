# Case study: DevTool Show HN → 280 points, 4.2k sessions (anonymous)

## Context
B2B CLI tool, OSS repo, 0 ad spend. Founder had 3 weeks HN comment history (no links).

## Tactic stack applied
1. `comm_ih_build_log` — two IH posts T-14, T-7
2. `comm_show_hn_submit` — Tuesday 9:15am PT
3. `comm_hn_comment_sla_60m` — 94% replies <60 min
4. `comm_teardown_metrics_d1` — public numbers

## Outcome
- 4h: 280 points, 62 comments
- 72h: 4,200 sessions (HN referrer), 380 signups (9%)
- 30d: 41 paid from HN cohort

## Lesson
First comment technical depth mattered more than title polish. Demo worked on mobile — 40% of clicks.
