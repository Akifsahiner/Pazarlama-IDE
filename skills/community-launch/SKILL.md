# Community Launch

Professional community launch coordination: Show HN title, live demo URL, comment SLA, cross-post spacing, and teardown metrics.

Use when ICP lives on HN/IH/Reddit and the product has a **working demo** (not vaporware).

## Process
1. Map ICP → communities (`comm_icp_community_map`).
2. Audit subreddit rules before any post (`comm_subreddit_rules_audit`).
3. Draft Show HN title + first comment story (`comm_show_hn_title_draft`).
4. Space cross-posts ≥48h (`comm_cross_post_spacing_48h`).
5. Reply every HN comment ≤60 min in first 4 hours (`comm_hn_comment_sla_60m`).

## Output
- Community launch calendar (T-14 → D+1)
- Paste-ready Show HN post + IH milestone
- Kill rules when engagement is dead or rules violated

## Ethics
- No vote brigading, no alt accounts, no "please upvote" on Reddit
- Story-first posts — link in comment or profile when rules require
