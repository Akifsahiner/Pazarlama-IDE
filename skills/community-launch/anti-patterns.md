# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Ask for upvotes on HN, Reddit, or Discord. **Why:** Brigading detection bans accounts and nukes threads. Ask for feedback on the product instead.
- **Don't:** Post the same copy to HN, Reddit, and IH within 24 hours. **Why:** Communities overlap; duplicate posts get flagged as spam.
- **Don't:** Use "We built an AI-powered platform" as Show HN title. **Why:** HN readers skip generic AI launches — name the outcome ("Spell-check for SQL queries").
- **Don't:** Link-drop without context on Reddit. **Why:** Instant removal + ban; subreddit mods share ban lists.
- **Don't:** Launch without a working demo URL. **Why:** HN traffic is one-shot; broken flows waste 5k–50k sessions permanently.
- **Don't:** Argue with critics in thread. **Why:** One factual reply; emotional defense tanks the post's perceived quality.
- **Don't:** Use alt accounts to upvote or comment. **Why:** HN and Reddit detect patterns; permanent reputation damage.
- **Don't:** Post in unrelated subreddits because they're large. **Why:** Wrong ICP = zero conversion and mod removal.
- **Don't:** Fake metrics in IH milestone posts. **Why:** IH community verifies; credibility loss is permanent.
- **Don't:** Schedule Show HN during US holidays or major conference keynotes. **Why:** Traffic competes with megathreads; you won't rank.
- **Don't:** Treat HN as ads channel. **Why:** Promotional tone gets flagged; educational/builder tone wins.
- **Don't:** Skip subreddit rules audit. **Why:** One rule violation = ban; can't retry that community for months.
- **Don't:** Ignore Dev.to / blog after HN spike. **Why:** Search-indexed companion content captures long-tail for months.
- **Don't:** Bundle multiple products in one Show HN. **Why:** Confuses readers; focus one sharp offer.
- **Don't:** Launch community and PH same day. **Why:** Split founder attention; pick one spike channel per 72h window.
