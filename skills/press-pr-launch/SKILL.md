# Press & PR Launch

Executable press coordination for **big launches only**: embargo datetime lock, 5 personalized reporter pitches, press kit PDF, approved founder quote, and 6-hour launch monitoring.

Use when `days_until_launch ≥ 7` and you have real proof (customer metric, funding, partnership, or named design partner) — not for minor feature drops.

## When to apply

- Major version, public launch, funding announcement, or category-defining product
- Founder can approve quotes and assets within 48h
- Launch datetime is fixed and shareable under embargo

## When to skip

- Stealth mode, no demo, or <7 days to launch without assets
- No news hook and no proof — use community or PH skills instead
- Enterprise-only ICP with no trade press fit

## Playbook routing

| Profile | Playbook |
|---------|----------|
| No press relationships, cold | `no-audience` — build runway; honest 0–1 pickup ceiling |
| Warm list + some prior coverage | `with-email-list` — amplify pickups, not replace pitches |
| B2B / devtool ICP | `b2b-saas` — trade press + practitioner angle |
| ≥14d runway + proof + assets ready | `aggressive-press-war-room` |

## Core deliverables

1. **Embargo datetime** — locked in writing with every reporter (`pr_embargo_datetime_lock`)
2. **Press kit PDF** — one-pager + screenshots + founder headshot (`pr_press_kit_pdf`)
3. **5 reporter pitches** — each with personalized hook (`pr_reporter_shortlist_5`, `pr_pitch_personalized_hook`)
4. **Founder quote** — pre-approved, on-message, ≤60 words (`pr_founder_quote_approved`)
5. **Customer proof permission** — named metric or logo only with written OK (`pr_customer_proof_permission`)

## Honest ceiling

Without existing reporter relationships, expect **0–1 organic pickups** on a cold list of 5. Trade and vertical outlets beat generic "tech startup launches" for unknown brands. Aggressive means preparation intensity — not guaranteed TechCrunch.

## Primary metric

`press_pickups_embargo_window` — published or syndicated stories that cite your launch between embargo lift and +48h.

## Anti-patterns to enforce

Never blast identical pitches, miss embargo times, fabricate customer logos, or pay for pay-for-play "articles." See `anti-patterns.md`.
