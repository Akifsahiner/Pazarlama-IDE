# Template: Reporter pitch (personalized)

Send from founder or founder-addressed alias. **One reporter per email.** No attachments >5MB — link to press kit PDF.

---

## Subject lines (pick one pattern)

```
{{reporter_first_name}}, embargoed: {{company}} — {{news_hook_in_8_words}}
```

```
Story idea (embargo {{date}}): {{category}} teams cut {{metric}}
```

```
Following your {{recent_article_topic}} piece — {{one_line_angle}}
```

---

## Body

```
Hi {{reporter_first_name}},

I read your {{recent_article_title}} on {{outlet}} — especially {{specific_point_you_agree_or_extend}}.

I'm {{founder_name}}, founder of {{product_name}}. We're announcing {{news_hook — funding / launch / partnership / metric}} on {{launch_date_human}}, under embargo until {{launch_datetime_utc}} ({{timezone_label}}).

**Why your readers might care:** {{one_sentence_icp_pain_and_outcome}}

**Proof point:** {{one_verifiable_metric_or_named_customer_with_permission}}

**Under embargo:** {{two_sentence_product_description — outcome not buzzwords}}

Press kit (PDF): {{press_kit_url}}
Demo: {{demo_url}}?utm_source=press_pitch

Happy to offer:
- {{founder_name}} for a 15-min pre-brief ({{two_time_windows}})
- Early access for fact-checking
- Named customer intro if helpful (with permission)

Approved quote for your use:
"{{founder_quote_60_words_max}}"
— {{founder_name}}, {{title}}

Let me know if this fits your beat or if you'd prefer I not follow up.

Best,
{{founder_name}}
{{title}}, {{company}}
{{phone_optional}}
```

---

## Follow-up (once, +48h if no reply)

```
Hi {{reporter_first_name}} — quick bump on the {{product_name}} embargo ({{launch_datetime_utc}}). Totally fine if this isn't a fit; won't follow up again.

Press kit: {{press_kit_url}}

{{founder_name}}
```

---

## Personalization checklist (per reporter)

- [ ] Cited a **specific** recent story (title + point)
- [ ] Hook matches **their beat** (not generic "startup")
- [ ] Embargo datetime identical to kit
- [ ] Body is **unique** — not copy-paste from other 4 pitches
- [ ] Proof point is verifiable
- [ ] No "I'd love to pick your brain" or "revolutionary AI"

---

## Do not

- Attach 25MB zip
- Offer pay-for-play or quid-pro-quo
- Promise exclusive to multiple outlets
- Send before press kit PDF is final
- Pitch tier-1 without relationship unless funding/category story is genuine
