# Playbook: Aggressive Press War Room

Maximum ethical intensity for coordinated press launch: embargo lock, 5 personalized pitches, press kit PDF, founder quote, 6-hour monitoring, 24h follow-up SLA. Use only when **proof + ≥14 days runway** are real — and at least one warm reporter path or exceptionally strong news hook.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Big launch profile: `days_until_launch ≥ 14` and documented proof (funding, metric, partnership, category first)
- [ ] Press kit PDF draftable in ≤5 business days (screenshots, quote, bio, embargo footer)
- [ ] Founder calendar: blocked 6h on embargo lift day + available for fact-check calls
- [ ] ≥1 warm reporter intro **or** hook strong enough to cold-pitch tier-2/ trade with honest 1–3 pickup ceiling
- [ ] Customer proof permission secured for any named logo/metric in kit
- [ ] Internal embargo policy signed (no LinkedIn/X/email leaks pre-lift)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | <14d runway or no proof | Postpone or `no-audience` standard track |
| standard | Proof + 14d, mostly cold reporters | 5 pitches, modest monitoring |
| aggressive | Proof + warm path + assets locked | This playbook — hour-by-hour war room |

**Honest ceiling:** Cold-only list → still 0–1 pickups; aggressive means **preparation intensity**, not guaranteed TechCrunch. Warm intro + funding can reach 3–8 pickups with flawless execution.

## Tactic stack

1. **`pr_press_kit_pdf` (T-14→T-7)** — ≤8 pages: boilerplate, fact sheet, screenshots, quote, embargo footer, media contact. Metric: PDF v1.0 by T-7, link tested.
2. **`pr_embargo_datetime_lock` (T-14)** — Same `launch_datetime_utc` in pitches, kit, internal brief. Metric: zero ambiguous "morning of" language.
3. **`pr_reporter_shortlist_5` (T-21→T-7)** — Five beat-matched reporters; mix of trade + tier-2 unless tier-1 relationship exists. Metric: shortlist with story citations.
4. **`pr_pitch_personalized_hook` (T-5)** — Unique body per reporter: their recent story + your news + embargo offer. Metric: 5/5 sent; 0 template duplicates.
5. **`pr_founder_quote_approved` (T-10)** — ≤60 words; approved in writing; included in kit and pitch P.S. Metric: quote unchanged in all materials.
6. **`pr_screenshot_asset_kit` (T-14)** — Hero, workflow, proof visual; dark mode if devtool. Metric: embedded in PDF + `/press/` folder.
7. **`pr_launch_monitoring_6h` (H0–H+6)** — Google News, outlet RSS, X lists, referral UTMs at H+1, H+3, H+6. Metric: monitoring sheet complete.
8. **`pr_follow_up_sla_24h` (T-5→D+2)** — Every reporter reply answered ≤24h; fact-check calls prioritized. Metric: 100% SLA.
9. **`pr_kill_no_pickup_48h` (H+48)** — Zero stories → stop outbound; owned teardown + email amplify. Metric: pivot decision documented.
10. **`pr_honest_coverage_ceiling` (T-21)** — Team briefed on realistic pickups before war room starts. Metric: ceiling in launch doc signed by founder.
11. **`pr_customer_proof_permission` (T-10)** — All logos/metrics/quotes permissioned. Metric: permission archive linked in internal doc.

## Orchestration

- **Primary:** Five reporter relationships + embargo-coordinated lift
- **Parallel (post-lift only):** Email list waves, founder LinkedIn, PH/community ≥24h after lift if same news
- **War room roles:** Founder (fact-check + quote), marketing (monitoring + UTM), optional AE (inbound spike)
- **Do not:** Wire services, paid articles, 50-reporter blasts, social leaks, conflicting exclusives

## Realistic outcomes

| Profile | Pickups (48h) | Press sessions (72h) | Signups | Notes |
|---------|---------------|----------------------|---------|-------|
| Aggressive cold (strong hook) | 0–1 | 0–1.5k | 0–150 | Execution still valuable |
| Aggressive + 1 warm intro | 1–3 | 1k–5k | 100–500 | Intro is the multiplier |
| Funding + 2 warm + trade | 3–8 | 5k–25k | 500–2k | Rare for early-stage |

Measure pipeline 14–60d for B2B; day-one traffic ≠ revenue.

## Kill / pivot rules

- T-7: kit not ready → delay pitches 7d; never send WIP PDF
- T-3: 0/5 replies → rewrite hook once; do **not** expand to 30 reporters
- H0: founder leak detected → acknowledge internally; do not blame reporters publicly
- H+48: 0 pickups → `pr_kill_no_pickup_48h`; no paid placement escalation
- Story runs with wrong metric → correction request within 4h; update kit immediately

## Ethics line

- **Never:** pay-for-play, fabricated proof, fake exclusives, embargo leaks, duplicate pitches disguised as personal
- **Always:** accurate facts, permissioned customers, respect reporter beat and deadline
- Aggressive = orchestration discipline — not manipulation or entitlement to coverage
