# Playbook: Press Launch for B2B SaaS

Tier-1 tech press ignores most B2B SaaS unless funding, category creation, or a hard metric breaks through. **Trade and vertical press** (DevOps, HR, fintech, security) convert better for practitioner ICP — plan pitches accordingly.

## Preconditions
- [ ] ICP validation: practitioners (ops, eng, RevOps) vs enterprise economic buyers
- [ ] At least one proof point buyers trust: named logo (with permission), before/after metric, or compliance milestone
- [ ] Practitioner-facing headline angle — not "enterprise digital transformation platform"
- [ ] Demo or signup live for press referral traffic

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Enterprise-only ICP, no practitioner user | Skip broad press; analyst briefings + LinkedIn |
| standard | Practitioner ICP + trade press fit | This playbook — vertical outlets first |
| aggressive | Funding + warm trade press + 14d runway | `aggressive-press-war-room` + parallel LinkedIn founder post post-lift |

## Timeline

| Phase | When | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| T-21 | −21d | Map trade press: 10 outlets where buyers read (not generic tech) |
| T-14 | −14d | Press kit with ROI metric slide + customer permission |
| T-10 | −10d | Brief sales/CS: inbound spike + correct pricing in talking points |
| T-7 | −7d | Shortlist 5 reporters (3 trade, 2 tech if hook supports) |
| T-5 | −5d | Pitches lead with buyer pain + metric, not feature list |
| H0 | Embargo lift | Sales stand-by; demo link with UTM in kit |
| H0–H+6 | Launch window | Monitor trade outlets first |
| D+1…D+30 | Pipeline | Attribute demos with `utm_source=press` + outlet field in CRM |

## Tactic stack

1. **`pr_honest_coverage_ceiling` (T-21)** — B2B cold: 0–1 trade pickup realistic; tier-1 requires funding or category story. Metric: ceiling in plan.
2. **`pr_customer_proof_permission` (T-14)** — Named logo or metric with buyer-relevant outcome. Metric: permission on file.
3. **`pr_screenshot_asset_kit` (T-14)** — Workflow screenshot practitioners recognize; admin UI if that's the buyer. Metric: 3 contextual shots.
4. **`pr_press_kit_pdf` (T-14)** — Include: problem stat, product fact sheet, integration logos, pricing **range** if public. Metric: PDF approved by founder + sales.
5. **`pr_founder_quote_approved` (T-10)** — Quote speaks to buyer pain, not "thrilled to launch." Metric: ≤60 words, no jargon soup.
6. **`pr_reporter_shortlist_5` (T-7)** — Prioritize trade beat reporters who covered 2+ competitors. Metric: 3/5 trade minimum.
7. **`pr_pitch_personalized_hook` (T-5)** — Hook = their last article's thesis + your contrarian metric. Metric: 5 unique bodies.
8. **`pr_embargo_datetime_lock` (T-5)** — Align with product/marketing launch if simultaneous. Metric: datetime in all materials.
9. **`pr_launch_monitoring_6h` (H0)** — Track trade RSS + Google News alerts for category terms. Metric: sheet at H+1/3/6.
10. **`pr_follow_up_sla_24h` (T-5→D+2)** — Fact-check calls include correct pricing/seats. Metric: pricing one-pager ready.
11. **`pr_kill_no_pickup_48h` (H+48)** — Pivot to founder LinkedIn practitioner post + customer case study. Metric: pivot logged.

## Orchestration

- **Primary:** Trade/vertical press pitches
- **Parallel:** LinkedIn founder post **post-embargo** with same metric as kit
- **Sales:** CRM field `lead_source_detail=press_{{outlet}}`; AE knows demo spike timing
- **Do not:** Pitch consumer tech angle to WSJ for a niche RevOps tool

## Realistic outcomes

| Profile | Pickups | Demos (14d) | Pipeline | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Cold B2B, strong metric | 0–1 trade | 5–15 | 1–3 opps | Quality > vanity tier |
| Warm trade intro + proof | 1–2 | 15–40 | 3–8 opps | Best ROI path |
| Tier-1 tech (no funding) | 0 | 0–5 | 0–1 | Don't budget time here |

Press sessions: 200–2k per trade pickup; tier-1 an order of magnitude higher but rare.

## Kill / pivot rules

- T-7: if no customer permission → remove logos from kit; delay pitch if story depends on them
- T-3: if only tech reporters on list and no funding hook → replace 3 with trade beats
- Demos are tire-kickers only → press angle misaligned with buyer; fix hook before next cycle
- Wrong pricing in story → immediate correction request; update kit within 24h

## Ethics line

- No inflated ROI claims without methodology
- No "stealth Fortune 500" without verifiable permission
- Sales must not offer reporters undisclosed affiliate or quid-pro-quo access
