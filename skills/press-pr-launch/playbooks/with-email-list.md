# Playbook: Press Launch With Email List Amplification

A warm email list **amplifies** press coverage — it does not replace reporter relationships. Use the list *after* embargo lifts to drive traffic to stories and your LP, never to scoop reporters or leak early.

## Preconditions
- [ ] ESP with segmentable engagement (opens/clicks)
- [ ] Press kit PDF + 5 reporter pitches in flight (see `no-audience` or `aggressive-press-war-room`)
- [ ] Embargo datetime locked; list team briefed on no pre-lift mentions
- [ ] UTM `utm_source=press` on LP + separate `utm_source=email_press_amplify` for list sends

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | List <1k or low engagement | Single post-lift email only |
| standard | 1–5k engaged | This playbook — 2 waves post-lift |
| aggressive | ≥5k engaged + press pickup | 3 waves + founder social **after** stories live |

## Timeline

| Phase | When | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| T-7 | −7d | Internal embargo brief: marketing must not mention launch date publicly |
| T-5 | −5d | Reporter pitches sent (parallel track) |
| T-1 | −1d | Pre-draft amplify email (do not schedule pre-lift) |
| H0 | Embargo lift | Confirm at least one story URL or proceed with owned launch narrative |
| H+2 | +2h | Wave 1: engaged segment — "as covered in {{outlet}}" or launch news |
| H+6 | +6h | Wave 2: general list + founder LinkedIn **only if** embargo clear |
| H+24 | +1d | Wave 3 (optional): cold segment / missed — different subject |
| H+48 | +48h | Thank-you + teardown metrics email |

## Tactic stack

1. **`pr_embargo_datetime_lock` (T-7)** — List owner signs embargo policy; no teaser with date. Metric: brief acknowledged.
2. **`pr_press_kit_pdf` (T-7)** — Link in amplify emails for sharers/bloggers (not a substitute for reporter kit). Metric: same PDF as press track.
3. **`pr_reporter_shortlist_5` (T-5)** — Email track does not replace pitches. Metric: 5 pitches sent before any list send.
4. **`pr_pitch_personalized_hook` (T-5)** — Hooks live before list mentions product news. Metric: pitch log predates email schedule.
5. **`pr_founder_quote_approved` (T-3)** — Quote reused consistently in email + social post-lift. Metric: same quote everywhere.
6. **`pr_launch_monitoring_6h` (H0–H+6)** — Watch for live URLs before hitting send on Wave 1. Metric: no email before lift.
7. **`pr_follow_up_sla_24h` (ongoing)** — Reporter questions trump email calendar. Metric: defer sends if fact-check pending.
8. **`pr_kill_no_pickup_48h` (H+48)** — No press pickup → email becomes owned launch story, not "as seen in TechCrunch." Metric: honest subject lines.

## Orchestration

- **Primary:** Reporter pitches + embargo discipline
- **Parallel (post-lift only):** ESP waves cite real outlet links when available
- **Do not:** "Exclusive preview" to list before embargo; subject lines claiming coverage that doesn't exist
- **PH/community:** Schedule ≥24h after embargo lift if same news cycle

## Realistic outcomes

| Profile | Pickups | List-driven sessions (48h) | Signups | Notes |
|---------|---------|--------------------------|---------|-------|
| 0 press + 3k list | 0 | 400–1.2k | 40–150 | Owned narrative still works |
| 1 pickup + 5k list | 1 | 1.5k–4k | 150–500 | Outlet link in email boosts both |
| 2+ pickups + 10k list | 2+ | 3k–10k | 300–1.2k | Message match critical |

Email amplifies; it rarely creates journalist interest alone.

## Kill / pivot rules

- If tempted to send pre-lift "something big coming" → stop; use waitlist nurture skill instead
- H0 with 0 live stories → Wave 1 is founder launch announcement, not faux press coverage
- Open rate <12% on amplify → fix list hygiene before Wave 3
- Complaint rate >0.1% → pause all waves

## Ethics line

- Never claim press coverage that didn't happen
- Never send list emails that break embargo timing
- Sharers get the same facts as reporters — no different metrics in email vs kit
