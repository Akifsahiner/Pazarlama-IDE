# Hook Variants — Short-Form Video

First 3 seconds decide everything. No logo intro. No "hey guys." Hook on screen AND in spoken word. Test 3+ formulas before scaling one format.

---

## Formula 1 — Negative outcome (best for problem-aware ICP)

**Visual:** Face close-up or text overlay on black.
**Spoken + text overlay:**
```
You're losing [metric/money/time] every week because of [specific mistake].
```

**Variants to A/B:**
- "Your [ICP role] team is bleeding [metric] — and it's not their fault."
- "Stop doing [common practice]. It's costing you [specific number]."
- "We wasted [$X / N hours] on [mistake] so you don't have to."

---

## Formula 2 — Before/after split (best for visual products)

**Visual:** Split screen — left "before" (chaos/spreadsheet), right "after" (clean product UI).
**Spoken + text overlay:**
```
[Boring task] used to take [X hours]. Now it takes [Y minutes].
```

**Variants:**
- "This is [task] without [product]. This is with."
- "[ICP] in 2024 vs 2025 — watch the difference."
- "POV: you finally fixed [pain]."

---

## Formula 3 — Contrarian claim (best for cold reach)

**Visual:** Founder direct to camera, slight lean-in.
**Spoken + text overlay:**
```
Unpopular opinion: [common advice] is wrong for [ICP].
```

**Variants:**
- "Everyone says [X]. That's why [ICP] stays stuck."
- "The [tool/category] industry doesn't want you to know this."
- "I got fired for saying this — but [contrarian truth]."

---

## Formula 4 — POV / identity (best for prosumer)

**Visual:** Over-shoulder screen or day-in-the-life cut.
**Spoken + text overlay:**
```
POV: You're a [identity] who [aspirational action].
```

**Variants:**
- "Things [identity] do that [amateurs] don't."
- "Day in the life of a [identity] using [workflow]."
- "Tell me you're [identity] without telling me."

---

## Formula 5 — Demo-first (best when product is self-explanatory)

**Visual:** Screen recording starts immediately — cursor action in frame 1.
**Spoken + text overlay:**
```
Watch me [specific task] in [timeframe].
```

**Variants:**
- "I built this in [time]. Here's it working."
- "No tutorial — just [outcome] in 15 seconds."
- "This button replaced our entire [process]."

---

## Hook testing matrix

Film one core script with 3 different hooks. Publish on staggered days. Track separately:

| Hook type | 3-sec retention target | Completion target | Profile click target |
|-----------|------------------------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Negative outcome | 65%+ | 40%+ | 2%+ |
| Before/after | 70%+ | 45%+ | 3%+ |
| Contrarian | 60%+ | 35%+ | 1.5%+ |
| POV | 55%+ | 30%+ | 1%+ |
| Demo-first | 75%+ | 50%+ | 4%+ |

**Scale rule:** Double down when 3-sec retention ≥ target on 2 of 3 tests.
**Kill rule:** Retention < 45% after 3 posts with same hook — rewrite, don't tweak music.

---

## Reject the hook if it:

- Opens with logo animation or "welcome back"
- Takes > 3 seconds to state the payoff
- Uses vague pain (" struggling with productivity") instead of specific ("14-tab spreadsheet for campaign reporting")
- Promises outcomes the product can't deliver in the demo that follows

## Ethical notes

- "I got fired" and fake urgency hooks erode trust if fabricated
- Before/after must reflect typical results — add "results vary" in caption if regulated
- Don't use competitor logos in split screen without legal clearance
