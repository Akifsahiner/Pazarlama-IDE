# Playbook: Short-Form Video With an Email List

Your list seeds the first views, comments, and shares — algorithm boost needs engagement in the first hour.

- **Days 1–2: Announce video series to list.** Subject: "We're posting daily build clips — want the unlisted link first?" Segment engaged subscribers; they comment in minute one when video goes public.
- **Days 3–7: Email unlisted links 2h before public post.** VIP early access drives replies ("saw it early") that become public comments when you flip to public. Include reply prompt: "What should we cover next?"
- **Days 8–14: Repurpose top email topics into hooks.** Highest-clicked email subjects → video hooks. List tells you what resonates before you film.
- **Days 15–21: Clip customer/list testimonials.** Ask list: "30-sec Loom on why you use [Product]?" Feature 3 clips as social proof videos. List becomes content factory.
- **Days 22–28: Cross-post winners back to list.** "This clip hit 50k views — here's the longer story." Email drives second wave of views; algorithm sees returning traffic.
- **Days 29–30: Measure signup attribution from video UTMs vs email.** Compare channels; keep format only if video UTMs beat email baseline or supplement it.

Track: 3-sec retention, comments in first hour, profile clicks, `utm_source=short_video` signups.
