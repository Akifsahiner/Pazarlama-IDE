# Playbook: Short-Form Video With No Audience

Zero followers means the algorithm is your only distribution. Volume + hook testing for 30 days beats one polished video.

- **Days 1–2: Create accounts, minimal bio.** Same handle across TikTok, Reels, Shorts. Bio: "Building [Product] in public | [ICP outcome]." Link to waitlist or landing with `?utm_source=tiktok`. No perfect branding — ship.
- **Days 3–4: Script 10 videos from templates.** (1) "Day 1 building [Product]" — founder face, 20 sec, (2) "Unpopular opinion: [ICP pain]" — 25 sec, (3) Screen recording problem → your solution sketch, 30 sec, (4) "Things I wish I knew as a [ICP role]" — list format, 35 sec, (5) Trending sound + text overlay about your niche — 15 sec. Hook-first on every script.
- **Days 5–6: Film all 10 in one session.** Phone tripod, window light. Founder talks to camera for hooks; screen record for demos. Imperfect is fine — authenticity beats polish at 0 followers.
- **Days 7–9: Edit and schedule 3/week for 3 weeks.** CapCut: auto-captions, cut dead air, 9:16 export. Schedule via platform native or Later/Buffer. Post Tue/Thu/Sat 8am or 12pm local — test both.
- **Days 10–14: Engage 30 min/day.** Comment on 10 videos from ICP creators (not "great post" — add value). Reply to every comment on your videos. Follow 20 accounts in niche. Algorithm favors active accounts.
- **Days 15–18: First analytics review.** Which 3-sec retention >40%? Which completion >25%? Reshoot 5 new videos copying winning hook structure only. Change one variable: same hook type, different example.
- **Days 19–22: "Build in public" series.** "Day 19: Shipped [feature]. Here's what broke." Weekly episodic hooks build return viewers. Number the series in caption.
- **Days 23–26: Reply-video format.** Even with 5 comments, turn each into a 15-sec reply video. "Someone asked how we handle [X] — here's 20 seconds." Drives comment loop.
- **Days 27–30: CTA test batch.** Same content, 3 different CTAs: "Link in bio," "Comment WAITLIST," "Follow for part 2." Measure profile clicks. Lock winning CTA for month 2.
- **Day 30+: Only continue if hook retention >35% on 3+ videos.** Otherwise pivot format (more face vs more screen, shorter vs longer) before quitting channel.

Realistic outcome: 12–15 videos, 200–2k total views, 1 video possible breakout (5k+), 20–80 waitlist/signups, 100–400 followers. Month 2 is when compounding starts if hook rate holds.
