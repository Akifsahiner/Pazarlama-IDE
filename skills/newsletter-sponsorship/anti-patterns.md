# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Sponsor broad "tech news" digests because subscriber count is huge. **Why:** ICP dilution yields 0.2% CTR and $500+ CPA. **Cost:** Budget gone before you learn anything.
- **Don't:** Run newsletter sponsorship without UTM parameters on every link. **Why:** You cannot attribute signups or enforce kill rules. **Cost:** Repeat spend on losers; kill winners by accident.
- **Don't:** Use display-ad copy ("Transform your workflow with our AI platform"). **Why:** Newsletter readers skim native blocks; promotional tone is invisible. **Cost:** <0.3% CTR; publisher may decline renewal.
- **Don't:** Book 4 newsletters in the same calendar week on first test. **Why:** Attribution overlap and founder bandwidth collapse. **Cost:** No clear winner; all slots underperform.
- **Don't:** Skip media kit review and sponsor examples. **Why:** Stale open rates and hidden "sponsor fatigue" policies waste slots. **Cost:** Pay for dead list or wrong audience geography.
- **Don't:** Exceed 120 words in primary sponsor block. **Why:** Most dev newsletters truncate or charge premium for long copy. **Cost:** CTA cut off; clicks die.
- **Don't:** Rebook the same newsletter 4 weeks in a row without a skip issue. **Why:** Repeat exposure without novelty → banner blindness. **Cost:** CTR halves by week 3.
- **Don't:** Ignore 7-day CPA kill rule because "brand awareness." **Why:** Dev newsletters are performance channels when UTM'd — awareness without signups is mispositioned spend. **Cost:** Burn runway on non-converting lists.
- **Don't:** Send broken or mobile-broken landing URLs. **Why:** Newsletter traffic is mobile-heavy (60%+); 404 or slow LP kills conversion in one send. **Cost:** Permanent reputation hit with publisher.
- **Don't:** Fake "as seen in" or imply editorial endorsement without disclosure. **Why:** Publishers require "Sponsored" labels; FTC and reader trust matter. **Cost:** Slot cancelled; brand damage in dev community.
- **Don't:** Negotiate only on CPM without asking sponsor-slot CTR history. **Why:** Cheap slot on low-engagement placement is expensive at any price. **Cost:** Low clicks → infinite CPA.
- **Don't:** Use the same UTM campaign for HN, PH, and newsletter in one week. **Why:** Channel collision makes teardown lies. **Cost:** Wrong kill/scale decisions.
- **Don't:** Sponsor during major newsletter holidays without checking issue calendar. **Why:** Thanksgiving, Christmas, August lull drops opens 30–50%. **Cost:** Pay full price for half the eyeballs.
- **Don't:** Book without confirming link policy (one link vs multiple). **Why:** Some newsletters allow only one URL; your brief may be rejected day before send. **Cost:** Rush rewrite or forfeited slot.
- **Don't:** Treat newsletter sponsorship as substitute for Product Hunt or paid search. **Why:** Different intent and measurement — sponsorship is mid-funnel dev awareness with CPA accountability. **Cost:** Wrong expectations and channel conflict same launch week.
