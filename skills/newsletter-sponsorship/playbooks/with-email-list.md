# Playbook: With Email List

You have ≥500 own subscribers (waitlist, beta, or product updates). Use list proof in sponsor copy and coordinate timing so newsletter traffic lands on warm social proof — not a cold LP.

- **T-21: Mine list for proof points.** Count beta users, NPS snippet (with permission), or "X teams use this for Y" — honest numbers only.
- **T-14: Shortlist newsletters where your subscribers already read** (poll 50 users: "Which newsletters do you read?").
- **T-10: Native copy references community** — "500 devs on our waitlist asked for Z" beats generic feature list.
- **T-7: Email your list a heads-up** — not "click our sponsor ad" but "We're sponsoring [Newsletter] this week — would love your feedback if you see us."
- **H0: Sponsor send** — UTM `utm_content=hook_a`.
- **H+3: Internal email** to list with teardown invite ("Saw us in React Status? Tell us what confused you").
- **D+7: Teardown** comparing newsletter signups vs organic list growth same week.

Do **not** ask list to click sponsor link artificially. Do ask for product feedback if they arrive organically.

## Preconditions
- [ ] ≥500 subscribers with ≥25% open rate on last send (or honest waitlist size)
- [ ] LP shows social proof aligned with sponsor copy
- [ ] UTM scheme excludes owned-email traffic (`utm_medium` distinct)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | List <500 or cold | Single sponsor slot; list as research only |
| standard | 500–3k engaged | 2 newsletters over 4 weeks; proof in copy |
| aggressive | 3k+ with quotes | 3 slots + A/B hooks; coordinate list mention |

## Timeline
- **T-21**: Subscriber newsletter poll + proof mining
- **T-14**: Shortlist overlap ≥4/5
- **T-10**: Creative brief with list-backed proof
- **T-7**: Lock UTM; heads-up email drafted (send H-1, not H0)
- **H0**: Sponsor issue drops
- **H+3**: Feedback ask to owned list
- **D+7**: Teardown + rebook decision

## Tactic stack
1. **`nl_sponsor_shortlist_niche`** — Poll-informed priority
2. **`nl_audience_overlap_score`** — Boost score if list overlap confirmed
3. **`nl_media_kit_request`** — 3 kits; prioritize list-mentioned names
4. **`nl_sponsor_creative_brief`** — Include proof points + disclosure
5. **`nl_native_copy_120_words`** — Community angle hook
6. **`nl_utm_campaign_scheme`** — Separate campaigns per newsletter
7. **`nl_slot_booking_lead_time`** — Stagger slots 7d apart minimum
8. **`nl_creative_ab_hook`** — Hook A week 1, Hook B week 3 (different list)
9. **`nl_post_sponsor_teardown`** — Compare list vs sponsor cohort quality

## Orchestration
- Owned email supports credibility; never click-farm sponsor links
- Sponsor weeks avoid heavy promotional blasts to same ICP
- One primary newsletter per 7d window

## Realistic outcomes
- 500–3k list + strong newsletter fit: 50–90 signups/slot; higher activation if LP shows same proof
- Mismatched proof (enterprise list, consumer newsletter) → high CTR, low activation

## Kill / pivot rules
- Newsletter signups activate <15% at D+14 → fix onboarding before next slot
- List poll shows <10% read target newsletter → deprioritize that title
- CPA > ceiling at D+7 → kill; do not compensate with list blast

## Ethics line
- No instructing subscribers to click sponsor URL
- Honest metrics in copy; permission for quotes
