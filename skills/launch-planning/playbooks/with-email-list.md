# Playbook: Launching With an Existing Email List

You have a warm asset most founders don't. The list is the lever — design the plan to detonate it, then convert overflow from earned channels.

- **Day −30 to −14: Warm the list.** Send 2 "behind the scenes" emails: one teasing the problem, one introducing a beta cohort. Goal: 20–30% open rate on the second send, 5–10% click to a signup-for-early-access page.
- **Day −14: Segment the list.** Tag respondents as `power-user`, `interested`, `cold`. You'll send 3 different launch sequences.
- **Day −7: Send the "we're launching next week" email** with the specific day and a 1-click "remind me" button. Open rates here predict launch-day conversions ±20%.
- **Day −2: Email power-users + interested with the ask**: "We're launching on Product Hunt on [day]. If you find it useful, an honest comment helps us a lot. Here's the link 8 hours before it goes live." Never ask for upvotes directly (PH penalizes this).
- **Launch Day, T-0 (12:01am PT): PH submission live.** Send email to power-user segment immediately, interested segment at 7am PT, cold segment at 11am PT. Staggering avoids ESP throttling and spreads PH velocity over 6+ hours (which the algorithm rewards).
- **Launch Day, T+3h: First X/LinkedIn thread**, quoting your own list response ("woke up to 412 PH comments…"). This converts your owned momentum into earned reach.
- **Day +1: "Thank you" email** with a 24-hour-only launch discount or bonus. Captures the fence-sitters.
- **Days +2 to +7: Daily public update** with metrics, lessons, and 1 customer story. Each one is a re-pitch to the press, partners, and your list.

Realistic outcome with a 5–20k engaged list: 5–12% list-to-signup, top 3 on PH, 30–80 inbound press/partner DMs.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for launch-planning
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
