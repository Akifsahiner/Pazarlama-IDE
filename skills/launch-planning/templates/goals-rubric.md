# Launch Goals Rubric (Copy & Use)

Use this to set a primary KPI, secondary KPIs, and an explicit success/failure threshold before launch. Print this. Tape it above the monitor.

---

## 1. The Primary KPI

> **On [launch date], success = [number] of [thing] within [window].**

Pick **one**. Examples:
- "By Friday of launch week, 750 qualified signups (work email, ICP-matched)."
- "Within 30 days, 40 paying customers at ≥$49/mo."
- "On Day 1, top-3 on Product Hunt AND 100 inbound demo requests."
- "Within 14 days, 25 named-logo enterprise demos booked."

**Reject the KPI if it:**
- Has no number
- Has no deadline
- Has no qualifier ("signups" → "qualified signups from ICP")
- Can't be measured by an existing tool

---

## 2. Secondary KPIs (pick 3, no more)

Choose from this set — they're proven leading indicators:

| KPI | Healthy band (early-stage launch) |
|---|---|
| Landing page visitor → signup rate | 4–10% during launch window |
| PH product page upvotes (Day 1) | Top 5: 600+; Top 1: 1,500+ |
| Hacker News front page (top 30) | 200+ upvotes in first 4 hours |
| Launch tweet impressions | 100k+ for founder with 5k followers |
| Press mentions (tier 1 + tier 2) | 2–5 mentions in launch week |
| Free → paid conversion (week 4) | 3–8% for self-serve SaaS |
| Email list growth during launch | +25–60% of pre-launch size |
| Activation rate (signup → core action) | >40% within 24h |

---

## 3. Success Tiers (commit BEFORE launch)

Write the exact numbers — not aspirations — into each tier:

- **Knock-out:** _______________ → triggers a v2 launch in 60 days + paid amplification
- **Solid:** _______________ → continue plan as-is, plan adjacent feature launch in 90 days
- **Acceptable:** _______________ → pause paid, double down on content + community
- **Flop:** _______________ → soft-relaunch in 90 days with revised positioning. No panic decisions for 7 days.

---

## 4. Pre-Mortem (write this before, not after)

- **If we hit knock-out, the most likely reason will be:** ________________
- **If we flop, the most likely reason will be:** ________________
- **The one assumption we're betting the launch on is:** ________________
- **The thing we'll regret not preparing is:** ________________

---

## 5. Anti-goals (write what you will NOT chase)

Examples:
- "We will NOT chase #1 on PH at the cost of ICP focus."
- "We will NOT discount below $39/mo to inflate paid count."
- "We will NOT pitch press outlets whose audience isn't our buyer."

Anti-goals prevent launch-week panic decisions. They protect the strategy from the dopamine.
