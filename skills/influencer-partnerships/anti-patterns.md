# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Pay for followers or fake engagement pods through creators. **Why:** Inflated metrics, zero conversion, platform bans. Audit engagement rate (comments/likes ratio) and comment quality before signing.
- **Don't:** Send a 10-page brand deck as the "brief." **Why:** Creators ignore it. One-pager: product, 3 talking points, do/don't, link, disclosure. Attach demo video or Loom.
- **Don't:** Require verbatim script reads. **Why:** Audiences detect sponsored tone instantly. Provide bullet points; approve draft before publish if needed, but preserve creator voice.
- **Don't:** Run one-off posts without tracking links or codes. **Why:** You can't optimize or cut losers. Every partnership gets unique UTM + promo code from day one.
- **Don't:** Partner with creators whose audience doesn't match ICP. **Why:** Vanity views, zero pipeline. Sample 20 comments on their last 5 posts — are they your buyers or random engagement?
- **Don't:** Skip the exclusivity window in competitive categories. **Why:** Creator posts about your competitor the next week; your spend is wasted. 30–90 day category exclusivity in contract.
- **Don't:** Ghost creators after the campaign. **Why:** Best partners come from relationships. Debrief, pay on time, offer renewal before they pitch competitors.

- **Don't:** Ship generic advice untied to this product profile. **Why:** Founders already have ChatGPT. **Cost:** Trust and retention.

- **Don't:** Recommend five channels at once. **Why:** Dilutes learning. **Cost:** No clear kill signal.

- **Don't:** Invent metrics or connector data. **Why:** Honest measurement is a product promise. **Cost:** Credibility collapse.

- **Don't:** Skip acceptance criteria on tasks. **Why:** Ambiguous done. **Cost:** Endless 'almost shipped'.

- **Don't:** Hide tradeoffs. **Why:** Senior operators state ceilings. **Cost:** Overpromise churn.
