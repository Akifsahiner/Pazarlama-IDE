# Playbook: Influencer Partnerships for B2B SaaS

B2B influencer GTM targets *practitioners and thought leaders*, not lifestyle creators. Podcast hosts, newsletter writers, and LinkedIn/X operators in your vertical are the buy.

- **Day −21: Build creator target list (30 names).** Criteria: 5k–100k followers, posts weekly in your category, audience comments show ICP titles. Sources: SparkToro, Listen Notes (podcasts), Substack leaderboards, LinkedIn search. Score: audience fit (1–5), engagement rate, past brand deals.
- **Day −18: Draft the one-page influencer brief.** Sections: Product (2 sentences), ICP, 3 talking points (pain → solution → proof), Do (show real product, share personal use case) / Don't (read script, over-promise), Disclosure (#ad required), Assets (demo Loom, logos, tracking link template).
- **Day −14: Outreach wave 1 — 10 micro-creators.** DM or email: "Love your [specific post/episode] on [topic]. We built [Product] for [ICP]. Would a free lifetime account + $300–500 for an honest review post fit your content?" Personalize first line; attach brief.
- **Day −10: Negotiate hybrid deals.** Structure: 60% upfront, 40% on 10+ qualified signups via their code. Provide unique code (CREATORNAME20) and UTM. 30-day category exclusivity in simple agreement.
- **Day −7: Ship creator kits.** Box or digital: brief PDF, demo account pre-loaded with sample data, 3 screenshot assets, founder availability for 15-min call if they want depth. Top creators get early feature access.
- **Day −3: Approve drafts (if requested).** Check: disclosure present, no false claims, link/code correct. Suggest one authentic personal angle — "What would you actually use this for?" — not corporate talking points.
- **Day 0: First posts go live (stagger 3–5 over 7 days).** Monitor comments; founder replies to questions within 2 hours on creator posts. Route signups with creator code to priority onboarding.
- **Days +1 to +7: Track attribution daily.** Dashboard: signups by code, qualified rate, trial starts. Pause outreach to new creators until first batch data is in. Kill CPA >$200; rebook CPA <$80.
- **Days +8 to +14: Podcast/newsletter wave.** Pitch 5 podcasts: guest spot (founder story) or host-read ad. Newsletter sponsors: 1 paragraph + link in 3 niche newsletters (e.g. Lenny's, category-specific). Brief same as social; longer format for depth.
- **Days +15 to +30: Renew top 2 performers.** Offer 3-month ambassador: 1 post/month + quarterly product input call. Rev-share bump on renewals. Turn best creator into case study ("How [Creator] uses [Product]").

Realistic outcome: 5–12 active creators, 150–500 qualified signups, $25–$70 blended CPA, 10–18% trial-to-paid, 2–3 ambassador renewals.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for influencer-partnerships
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
