# Influencer Brief — One-Pager Template

One page. Bullets, not script. Preserve creator voice — your job is clarity and compliance, not a teleprompter.

---

## Campaign header

```
Campaign:     [Product name] — [one-line positioning]
Brand:        [Company] | [website]
Contact:      [Name, role, email]
Live window:  [Earliest date] – [Latest date] (staggered preferred)
Deliverable:  [1 Reel / 1 YouTube integration / 1 newsletter mention / etc.]
Compensation: [Base fee / product-only / affiliate: X% or $X per signup]
Tracking:     UTM link + promo code [CODE] (creator-unique)
```

---

## The story (3 bullets max)

- **Problem:** [ICP] struggles with [specific pain in plain language]
- **Solution:** [Product] [mechanism] so [outcome]
- **Proof:** [One metric, customer name, or demo fact — e.g. "40 teams in beta, avg 4h saved/week"]

---

## Talking points — say it your way

Pick 2–3. Do not read verbatim.

1. [Specific use case — "When I [scenario], [product] [what it does]"]
2. [Differentiator — what incumbents or manual process get wrong]
3. [Honest limitation — "It's not for [edge case] yet; best if you [qualifier]"]

---

## Do

- [ ] Disclose partnership per FTC/ASA/local rules (#ad, "paid partnership," verbal disclosure in video)
- [ ] Show the product in use — screen recording or real workflow beats logo splash
- [ ] Use your unique link: `[creator UTM URL]` and code `[CODE]` for attribution
- [ ] Mention [free tier / trial terms] accurately — no lifetime deals unless confirmed below
- [ ] Send draft for factual review 48h before publish (not creative approval)

---

## Don't

- [ ] Claim [specific regulated outcome] unless we provide written substantiation
- [ ] Compare to [Competitor] by name without legal clearance
- [ ] Promise [feature] — it's on roadmap for [quarter], not live yet
- [ ] Run paid boost on the post without written approval (conflicts with whitelisting deals)
- [ ] Exclusivity in [category] applies for [30/60/90] days from publish date

## Required disclosure (copy-paste for caption/description)

**US (FTC):**
```
#ad — [Company] sponsored this post. I only partner with tools I actually use. Opinions are mine.
```

**UK (ASA):**
```
Ad | Paid partnership with [Company]
```

**Verbal (video, first 30 seconds):**
```
"[Company] sponsored this video — they paid me to share my honest experience."
```

## Approval workflow

1. Creator sends draft → factual review within 24h (business hours)
2. Creator publishes within [N] days of approval
3. Creator sends live URL within 1 hour of publish
4. Payment / affiliate activation within [N] days of live URL

---

## Reject the brief if it:

- Scripts word-for-word what the creator must say
- Omits disclosure requirements
- Promises performance ("you'll get 10k signups") to the creator
- Has no unique tracking before outreach sends

## Ethical notes

- Product-for-post only deals still require disclosure
- Micro-influencers deserve response within 48h — ghosting damages brand reputation in tight niches
- Right to decline edits that change creator opinion — brief covers facts, not sentiment
