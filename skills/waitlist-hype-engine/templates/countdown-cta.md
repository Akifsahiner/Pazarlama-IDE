# Countdown & Launch CTA — Templates

Use real dates only. Scarcity must be truthful — batch limits, time windows, and founding cohort caps should match what ops can deliver.

---

## Template 1 — Waitlist page hero (pre-launch)

**Headline:** [Outcome] for [ICP] — opening [month day].

**Subhead:** Join [N]+ [teams/founders] on the waitlist. Top referrers get first access.

**Primary CTA:** Join the waitlist →

**Secondary line (below form):**
```
Batch 1 opens [date]. Founding members (5+ referrals) get in 48 hours early.
```

**Live elements to include:**
- Signup counter: specific number, updates on submit
- Optional ticker: "Sarah from Notion just joined" (first name + company only, with consent)
- Referral rank shown post-signup, not pre-signup

## Template 2 — 48-hour countdown email

**Subject:** 48 hours — [product] opens for [cohort name]

```
Hey [first name],

Two days until your batch gets access.

[Date], [time] [timezone]: [product] opens for [Wave 1 / founding cohort / your tranche].

Before then:
1. Save this link: [launch URL] (bookmark it — we'll also email you the code)
2. Optional: share your referral link one last time to confirm founding status: [referral URL]
3. Block 20 minutes on [date] to set up — most teams are live in under 15

What you'll need:
- [Requirement 1, e.g. Google Workspace admin]
- [Requirement 2]
- No credit card for [free tier / trial length]

See you [day].

— [founder name]
```

## Template 3 — Launch day SMS / push (optional; high opt-in only)

```
[Product] is live for your batch. Your code: [CODE]. Link: [short URL]. Window closes [date]. — [founder first name]
```

Keep under 160 characters if SMS. Always include opt-out for SMS.

## CTA button copy — pick one

| Context | Button text | Avoid |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Cold traffic | Join the waitlist | Get early access (vague) |
| Warm / list email | Reserve my spot | Sign up now |
| Post-signup | Share to move up | Refer friends (sounds MLM) |
| Launch day | Start free → | Buy now (if free tier exists) |
| Batch full | Join Batch 2 waitlist | Notify me (passive) |

---

## Reject the countdown if it:

- Uses a timer that resets on every page load (dark pattern)
- Says "only 3 spots left" without a real cap
- Opens access before sending codes to the cohort (support nightmare)
- Promises lifetime pricing that legal hasn't approved

## Ethical notes

- If launch slips, email the list before the countdown hits zero
- Batch sizing should reflect onboarding capacity — don't open 10,000 slots if support handles 50/day
- Document timezone clearly; "midnight" without PT/ET confuses international waitlisters
