# Waitlist Drip Sequence — Outline

Weekly rhythm beats daily nagging. Every email must deliver value, proof, or urgency — never "we're still building." Minimum 21 days between waitlist open and first access.

---

## Sequence map (5 emails over 4 weeks)

| # | Timing | Purpose | Subject line pattern |
|---|--------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | Day 0 (confirm) | Set expectations + referral CTA | You're #[position] — here's what's next |
| 2 | Day 3 | Value / education | The [problem] playbook we use internally |
| 3 | Day 10 | Social proof wave | [N] teams joined — here's what they're saying |
| 4 | Day 17 | Behind-the-scenes | What we shipped this week (with screenshot) |
| 5 | Day 21–25 | Scarcity + launch tranche | Doors open [date] — your batch is [cohort name] |

---

## Email 1 — Confirmation (Day 0, immediate)

**Subject:** You're #[position] on the [product] waitlist

```
Hey [first name],

You're in. You're #[position] on the list.

What happens next:
- We send one email per week with progress, not spam
- First access goes to top referrers and founding cohort — details in email 2
- Launch window: [month]. You'll get 48 hours notice before your batch opens

Want to move up now? Share your link: [referral URL]

— [founder name]
P.S. Reply with your biggest [pain area] — I read every one.
```

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Referral link works on mobile
- [ ] Position number is accurate (or honestly rounded: "top 30%")
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present

---

## Email 2 — Value drop (Day 3)

**Subject:** The [specific tactic] that cut our [metric] by [X]%

```
Hey [first name],

Before [product] exists publicly, here's something useful on its own.

[2–3 paragraphs: a framework, checklist, or mini-case study related to the product's domain. No product pitch until the last line.]

Example structure:
- Paragraph 1: the mistake most [ICP] teams make
- Paragraph 2: the counter-intuitive fix (with one specific number)
- Paragraph 3: how to apply it this week

We're building [product] to automate step 2. You're #[position] on the list — share to move up: [referral URL]

— [founder name]
```

---

## Email 3 — Proof wave (Day 10)

**Subject:** [N] [ICP plural] on the list — 3 quotes from beta

```
Hey [first name],

Quick milestone: [N] [teams/founders/operators] are on the waitlist.

Three of them tested the beta last week:

"[Quote 1 — specific outcome, name + title]"
"[Quote 2]"
"[Quote 3]"

You're #[position]. Top 10% by referrals get access in the first tranche on [date].

Your link: [referral URL]

— [founder name]
```

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Quotes are real, attributed, and permission-granted
- [ ] Milestone number is specific (2,847 not "thousands")

---

## Email 4 — Build log (Day 17)

**Subject:** Shipped this week: [one concrete feature name]

```
Hey [first name],

Weekly build log — no fluff:

✓ Shipped: [feature] — [one sentence on what it does for the user]
✓ Fixed: [bug or pain point beta users reported]
→ Next: [upcoming item they'll care about]

[Screenshot or 15-second Loom GIF embedded]

Launch tranche 1 opens [date]. Founding cohort (5+ referrals) gets in [48h] early.

— [founder name]
```

---

## Email 5 — Scarcity + access (Day 21–25)

**Subject:** Your access window: [day], [time] [timezone]

```
Hey [first name],

We're opening [product] in batches starting [date].

Your batch: [Founding / Wave 1 / Wave 2]
Your window: [day] [time] – [day] [time] [timezone]
Your code: [will be sent 1 hour before — or embedded if single-use token]

What's included at launch:
- [Core capability 1]
- [Core capability 2]
- [Pricing: free tier / trial terms — no surprises]

If you're in the founding cohort (5+ referrals), check your inbox from yesterday — you're already in.

Questions? Reply here. I read everything until launch day.

— [founder name]
```

---

## Segment overrides

| Segment | Adjustment |
|---------|------------|
| 0 referrals, opened all emails | Add P.S. in Email 5: "Share once before [date] to jump one tranche" |
| 5+ referrals | Skip scarcity language; send "founding cohort" variant 48h early |
| Never opened | Resend Email 1 subject with "Did this land?" — one retry only |
| Unsubscribed | Honor immediately; no "we miss you" win-back during waitlist |

---

## Reject the drip if it:

- Sends more than 1 email per 5 days without explicit user opt-in to daily updates
- Uses fake countdown timers that reset on page refresh
- Contains zero value in emails 2–4 (all "coming soon")
- Announces launch date before it's locked internally
