# Referral Copy — Templates

Every referral touchpoint needs pre-filled copy that sounds like the sharer, not the brand. Write in first person. One idea per message. Include the unique referral link inline — never "click here."

---

## Template 1 — Confirmation page share block (default; show immediately after signup)

```
You're #[position] on the list.

Want to move up? Every friend who joins with your link bumps you ahead.

Your link: [referral URL]

Copy-paste any of these — or tap to share:

---

Twitter/X:
I've been waiting on [product] — it [one-line outcome]. Join with my link and we both skip ahead: [referral URL]

LinkedIn:
We're testing [product] before public launch. It [specific mechanism → outcome]. If you're [ICP role], worth getting on the list: [referral URL]

Slack / WhatsApp:
Hey — remember when we talked about [pain]? [Product] is opening soon. I signed up; if you use my link we both move up the queue: [referral URL]
```

**UX notes:**
- Show live rank update within 3 seconds of a successful referral
- Pre-fill the Twitter/X button with the tweet text above (don't make them compose)
- Never gate the referral link behind a second email confirm

## Template 2 — "Move up" email (send 24 hours after signup if referral count = 0)

**Subject:** You're #[position] — here's how to skip 200 spots

```
Hey [first name],

Quick update: you're #[position] on the [product] waitlist. We're letting people in in batches, starting with the top referrers.

Three people who joined yesterday moved into the top 100 by sharing once. Here's your link:

[referral URL]

What to send (copy-paste):

"I've been on the waitlist for [product] — it [outcome in 8 words]. Join with my link and we both move up: [referral URL]"

No spam required. One message to one person who actually has this problem is enough.

— [founder name]
```

## Template 3 — Top-referrer reward email (trigger at 5+ successful referrals)

**Subject:** You're in the founding cohort

```
Hey [first name],

You referred [N] people to [product]. That puts you in the top [X]% of the waitlist — and in our founding member cohort.

What that means:
- Access [48 hours / 1 week] before general waitlist
- Direct line to me for feedback (reply to this email)
- Your name on the founding members page (opt out anytime — just reply)

Your link still works if you want to keep climbing: [referral URL]

We're opening doors on [date]. I'll email you the night before with your access code.

— [founder name]
```

## Template 4 — Share asset one-liner (for OG image, bio link, signature)

Use on the auto-generated share card image footer:

```
[Product]: [Outcome] for [ICP]. I'm #[position] on the list — join and move us both up → [short link]
```

---

## Reject the referral copy if it:

- Promises cash rewards without legal review (affiliate disclosure, tax implications)
- Uses "viral," "explode your network," or MLM-adjacent language
- Hides the referral incentive — disclose that both parties benefit
- Requires sharing to 5+ platforms to unlock any reward (coercive; kills trust)
- Sounds like marketing wrote it — "We're thrilled you joined our community journey" → rewrite in first person

## Ethical notes

- Disclose referral mechanics on the waitlist page, not only in fine print
- Don't fake queue position or inflate counters — users compare notes
- If you cap founding cohort size, say so upfront; moving goalposts erodes trust
- GDPR/CAN-SPAM: referral emails must include unsubscribe; don't email referred users without their consent
