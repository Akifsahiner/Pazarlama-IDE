# Case study: Honest miss — K stuck at 0.12 (anonymous)

## Context
Consumer app, 50-person Discord, no product demo video. Ran referral loop anyway.

## Tactic stack applied
1. Referral unit shipped
2. ESP blast to Discord (too early)
3. K measured weekly

## Outcome
- 89 signups / 21d, K = 0.12 — unlock not desirable without demo
- Pivoted to demo-first landing; K rose to 0.22 in next 14d

## Lesson
Aggressive referral without proof asset fails — match dial to assets; honest ceiling stated upfront.
