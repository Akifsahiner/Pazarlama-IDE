# Playbook: Waitlist Hype Engine With No Audience

No list, no followers, no community. The waitlist *is* how you build the audience — but only if every signup is engineered to recruit the next one.

- **Day 1: Ship a one-page waitlist with a referral loop.** Headline: specific outcome, not category ("Stop losing leads in Slack threads" not "AI CRM"). Referral reward: skip 25 spots per share. No email required to see queue position after signup — reduces bounce.
- **Days 2–4: Personally recruit 15 seed signups.** DM 30 ICP matches on X or LinkedIn. Offer: "Free lifetime access if you're in the first 20 and refer 2 people." These 15 seed the social proof counter and referral graph.
- **Days 5–7: Build in public, one platform only.** Post daily: one build screenshot, one problem your ICP has, one "why we're building this." Every post ends with waitlist link + "Share to skip the line." Target: 300 followers, 50 waitlist signups from content.
- **Days 8–10: First micro-launch in 2 niche communities.** Indie Hackers build log, one subreddit or Slack where ICP congregates. Lead with story, not link. Include waitlist URL in profile/bio only. Target: 80–150 signups.
- **Days 11–14: Add live social proof to waitlist page.** Counter, recent signup ticker (first name only), "Join 247 others." Email signups: "You're #183 — move up by sharing." Measure referral rate; if <15%, A/B test reward (skip 10 vs skip 50 spots).
- **Days 15–18: Second wave — teaser asset.** Publish a 45-second screen recording (real UI, even rough). Post on X/LinkedIn + communities. Email waitlist: "First look at [Product]." Re-engage dormant signups; expect 10% open → 3% share.
- **Days 19–21: PH upcoming page goes live.** 7-day minimum before PH launch. Upcoming subscribers = free velocity. All traffic → same waitlist with referral defaults pre-loaded in share buttons.
- **Days 22–25: Recruit 10 "comment promise" supporters.** From top referrers and engaged DMs. Ask for honest PH comments on launch day — not upvotes. Real first-hour comments beat cold upvote rings.
- **Days 26–28: Scarcity + milestone email.** "500 on the list. Founding member pricing for first 100 activations." Include share assets: pre-written tweet, LinkedIn post, WhatsApp message.
- **Day 29: PH launch (Tuesday/Wednesday, 12:01am PT).** Waitlist stays open; PH drives spike. Confirmation emails to new signups include referral loop immediately.
- **Day 30+: Launch teardown post.** "How we got 400 waitlist signups with 0 followers." This is wave three — earns inbound and second referral cycle from readers who share the story.

Realistic outcome: 400–900 waitlist signups, 20–35% referral rate, K-factor 0.25–0.45, 500–1,500 PH visits, 35–50% activation from founding tranche.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for waitlist-hype-engine
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
