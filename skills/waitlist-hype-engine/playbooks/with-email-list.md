# Playbook: Waitlist With an Existing Email List

Your list is the seed audience — not the whole engine. Use it to jumpstart referral K-factor and social proof, then let the waitlist loop carry distribution.

---

## Day −28 to −21: Design the loop

- **Day −28:** Pick the referral unit. Default: queue position ("skip 500 spots per referral"). Write confirmation-page share copy using `templates/referral-copy.md`.
- **Day −27:** Build waitlist page: headline, live counter starting at 0, referral rank on confirm. Dedicated URL with UTM defaults for list traffic (`?utm_source=email&utm_medium=waitlist`).
- **Day −26:** Wire Email 1 (confirm) + referral link generation. Test on mobile — 60%+ of list traffic is phone.
- **Day −25:** Set K-factor baseline target: K ≥ 0.3 by Day 14 or fix the mechanic before scaling.
- **Day −24 to −21:** Internal dry run: 5 team members sign up, refer each other, verify rank updates and emails fire.

---

## Day −20: Soft open to power segment

- **Segment:** Opened 3 of last 5 emails OR clicked any link in last 90 days (~10–30% of list).
- **Email subject:** Something's coming — you're first in line
- **Body:** Tease the problem and outcome. No product name yet if pre-announce. CTA = "Get early access" → waitlist page.
- **Goal:** 8–15% click rate, 50–200 seed signups with referral links active.
- **Do not** email the full list yet — you need referral UX proven before volume hits.

---

## Day −14: First hype wave to full list

- **Email subject:** [Product name]: [outcome] — join the waitlist
- **Include:** Specific proof (beta quote, metric, or build screenshot). Referral mechanic explained in 2 sentences.
- **Founder sender** — not "Team @ Company."
- **Goal:** 20–35% open, 5–10% click, waitlist counter crosses 500+ (specific number on page).
- **Same day:** Founder LinkedIn post funneling to same URL. Cross-pollinate per principles — one link, tagged UTMs.

---

## Day −10: Value drip (not a pitch)

- Send Email 2 from `templates/drip-outline.md` — pure value content.
- **Goal:** Maintain 18%+ open rate. Replies are gold — respond personally to 20+ of them.
- **Tag** anyone who replies as `engaged-waitlist` for later founding cohort consideration.

---

## Day −7: Proof wave + milestone

- Send Email 3 with real signup count and 2–3 beta quotes.
- **Subject pattern:** [N] teams joined — here's what they're saying
- **Optional:** Update waitlist page ticker with recent signups (first name + company, consented).
- **Personal DMs:** Top 30 list members who haven't signed up — not via ESP. "We're opening waitlist — want your take before we go wide."

---

## Day −5 to −3: Referral push to non-sharers

- **Segment:** Signed up via list but 0 referrals.
- **Send:** "Move up" email from `templates/referral-copy.md` Template 2.
- **Parallel:** Email `engaged-waitlist` segment with behind-the-scenes build log (Email 4).
- **Goal:** Lift referral rate from ~5% to 15%+ before paid or earned traffic scales.

---

## Day −2: Founding cohort lock

- **Segment:** 5+ referrals → founding cohort email (Template 3).
- **Full list email (non-signups):** Last call — waitlist closes to Batch 1 on [date]. Countdown CTA from `templates/countdown-cta.md`.
- **Do not** promise founding status to new signups after this date if you announced a cap.

---

## Day −1: 48-hour countdown

- Send countdown email to all waitlisters (Template 2).
- Founding cohort receives access codes tonight — 48h early per promise.
- **Support prep:** Macro responses for "where's my code," timezone questions, referral disputes.

---

## Day 0: Launch tranche 1

- **6am local (founder's TZ):** Batch 1 codes to Wave 1 segment.
- **List email (signups only):** Your access window opens now — `[countdown-cta Template 5 T-0 variant]`
- **List email (non-signups, 4h later):** We're live — waitlist still open for Batch 2. Different subject from morning send.
- **Track:** Activation rate (signed up → logged in), referral K-factor for the week, support ticket volume.

---

## Day +1 to +7: Sustain the loop

- **Day +1:** Thank-you to list with launch metric screenshot ("847 teams in, 312 activated Day 1").
- **Day +3:** User story email — one customer, one metric, one quote.
- **Day +7:** Batch 2 announcement if Batch 1 hit capacity. Referrers from Batch 1 who didn't activate get a personal nudge.

---

## Realistic outcomes (5–20k engaged list)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| List → waitlist signup | 8–15% |
| Referral rate (≥1 referral) | 15–25% |
| K-factor by Day 14 | 0.3–0.6 |
| Tranche 1 activation | 40–60% of codes redeemed |

---

## Anti-patterns for list + waitlist

- Blasting the full list daily during waitlist — unsubscribes spike, K-factor drops
- Asking the list to share before you've sent one value email — feels extractive
- Different referral URLs per email without unified attribution — breaks rank tracking
- Opening Batch 1 larger than onboarding can support — NPS collapses before launch momentum compounds
