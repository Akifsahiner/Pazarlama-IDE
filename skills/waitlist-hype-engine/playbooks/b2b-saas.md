# Playbook: Waitlist Hype Engine for B2B SaaS

B2B waitlists convert on *credibility* and *access*, not FOMO alone. Design the loop around professional status and early-adopter advantage.

- **Day −28: Build the waitlist page.** Headline formula: "[Outcome] for [ICP] — without [pain]." Subhead: one proof point (logo strip or metric). CTA: "Join the founding cohort." Referral mechanic: move up 10 spots per qualified referral (same-domain email counts double).
- **Day −27: Wire the referral loop.** Unique referral codes, pre-filled LinkedIn share ("I'm #847 on the waitlist for [Product] — [one-liner]. Join me:"), confirmation page with live queue position. Test the full flow end-to-end.
- **Day −21: Seed with 20 design partners.** Personally invite ops/marketing leaders who match ICP. Offer founding-member pricing locked forever. Ask each to refer 2 peers in exchange for guaranteed week-one access.
- **Day −18: Publish founder LinkedIn post.** Format: problem story (3 paragraphs) → "We're building [Product]" → waitlist link. Pin comment with referral explainer. Target: 200–500 signups from post + comments.
- **Day −14: First hype wave — "Founding cohort preview."** Email waitlist with a 60-second Loom demo (real product, not mockup). Subject: "You're #412 — here's what we're building." Include share-to-move-up CTA. Expect 8–12% re-engagement.
- **Day −10: Launch PH upcoming page.** Same waitlist URL with `?ref=ph_upcoming` UTM. Cross-link from founder socials. PH subscribers auto-notify at launch; waitlist captures everyone else.
- **Day −7: Second hype wave — social proof push.** Update landing page with live counter, 3 named testimonials from design partners, and "847 teams waiting" ticker. Email: "We crossed 800 — share to move up."
- **Day −3: Segment top 10% referrers.** Send personal founder email: "You're in the first 50 to get access. Can you leave one honest comment on launch day?" These become your PH supporter comments and LinkedIn amplifiers.
- **Day −1: Scarcity email.** "Doors open tomorrow. Founding pricing ends in 72 hours." Include referral rank and final share assets (OG image, one-liner, 15-sec clip).
- **Day 0 (Launch): Open access in tranches.** Top referrers → full waitlist → public. Each tranche gets a "You're in" email with onboarding + second referral ask ("Now invite your team").
- **Days +1 to +7: Activation sprint.** Track waitlist-to-activation daily. Re-email non-activators with a 2-minute setup guide. Founding cohort who activate get featured on the "Built with [Product]" page.

Realistic outcome: 800–2,500 waitlist signups, 30–45% referral rate, K-factor 0.35–0.6, 45–55% activation within 7 days, 15–40 paying customers from founding cohort in 30 days.

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration

- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
