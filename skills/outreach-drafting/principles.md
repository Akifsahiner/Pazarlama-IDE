## Principles

1. **One human, one hook, one ask.** Each message gets a single personalization anchor, one value line, one low-friction CTA.
2. **Evidence-based personalization only.** Cite the lead's public post, job change, launch, or stack — never `{{firstName}}` theater.
3. **Drafts, never sends.** Output is for founder review; sending is explicit user action outside this skill.
4. **Three-touch max by default.** First touch + two follow-ups spaced 3–5 business days; more touches without engagement = spam.
5. **Honest claims trace to product profile.** No fabricated mutual connections, metrics, or "I saw you at [fake event]".
6. **Tone: peer, not vendor.** Write like a founder who built something relevant — not a SDR reading a script.
7. **Channel-native format.** Email ≠ LinkedIn DM ≠ Twitter DM — length, CTA, and proof differ per channel.
8. **Reply-rate beats cleverness.** Subject lines and first lines optimized for relevance, not puns.
9. **Follow-ups add value.** Each follow-up shares a new proof point or asset — never "just bumping this".
10. **Respect opt-out and regulations.** Include easy decline path mentally; no false urgency or manipulation.
