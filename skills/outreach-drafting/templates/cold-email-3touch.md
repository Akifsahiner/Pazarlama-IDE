# Cold Email — 3-Touch Sequence Template

One lead per sequence. Personalize every `[bracket]`. **Draft only — founder reviews before send.**

---

## Lead context block (fill first)

```
Lead:           [Name, Title @ Company]
Why-now:        [Trigger + source URL]
JTBD hook:      [Pain from research / their words]
Proof point:    [One evidence-backed outcome from product profile]
CTA type:       [15-min call / trial link / loom / reply-only]
already_subscriber: [true/false]
```

---

## Touch 1 — Relevance + one ask (Day 0)

**Subject options (pick one):**
- `[Company] + [pain noun — 3 words max]`
- `Re: [their post/article title truncated to 40 chars]`
- `Question about [their stated initiative]`

**Body:**

```
Hi [First name],

[One sentence referencing their specific public signal — post, job change, launch, hiring post. Quote or paraphrase with date if possible.]

[One sentence connecting to the problem your product addresses — use their language from JTBD, not your feature list.]

[One sentence proof: what it does + one metric or workflow. Example: "We help [ICP role plural] cut [task] from [X] to [Y] by [mechanism]."]

[One sentence CTA — low friction. Example: "Worth a 15-min compare-notes call this week? No deck."]

— [Your first name]
[Title], [Product]
```

**Touch 1 checklist:**
- [ ] First line is about them, not you
- [ ] No links in email 1 (optional: calendar link only if they requested)
- [ ] ≤120 words
- [ ] No "I hope this finds you well" / "I'm reaching out because"
- [ ] Subject ≤50 chars

---

## Touch 2 — New value (Day +4 business days)

**Subject:** `Re: [Touch 1 subject]` (same thread)

**Body:**

```
Hi [First name],

Quick follow-up with something concrete — [new asset: 90-sec loom / one-pager / specific workflow example / customer story].

[2–3 sentences describing what they'll see/learn. Specific workflow: "How [similar company type] automated [step] without replacing [tool they use]."]

If [pain from touch 1] is still on your radar, happy to walk through it. If not, no worries — I'll leave you alone after this.

— [Your first name]

[Optional link with UTM: ?utm_source=outreach&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=touch-2]
```

**Touch 2 checklist:**
- [ ] Adds NEW information (not "just bumping")
- [ ] Mentions touch-2 is second of two follow-ups
- [ ] Link goes to specific asset, not homepage

---

## Touch 3 — Graceful close (Day +9 business days)

**Subject:** `Re: [Touch 1 subject]`

**Body:**

```
Hi [First name],

Closing the loop — I know [pain area] competes with a hundred other priorities.

If timing opens up later, I'm here. Either way, [one genuine compliment about their work — specific post, company move, or public contribution].

— [Your first name]
```

**Touch 3 checklist:**
- [ ] No new pitch
- [ ] No urgency fabrication
- [ ] Explicit stop — do not send touch 4 without new trigger

## Example (filled — B2B SaaS)

**Touch 1 subject:** `RampRight + onboarding ramp`

```
Hi James,

Your post last week about cutting new-hire ramp from 14 to 5 days — especially the bit about managers rebuilding playbooks in Notion every quarter — stuck with me.

We built Closeflow for ops teams running that exact playbook problem at Series B SaaS: one source of truth for ramp milestones tied to Slack + HRIS, instead of scattered docs.

Teams our size typically see managers spend 6 fewer hours/week on ramp admin in the first month.

Worth 15 minutes to compare notes? No deck.

— Alex
Founder, Closeflow
```

---

## Bad vs good

| Bad | Good |
|-----|------|
| "I'd love to pick your brain" | Specific pain reference + concrete offer |
| Touch 2: "Just following up" | Touch 2: new loom, case study, or workflow |
| 300-word email | ≤120 words touch 1 |
| Fake mutual connection | Real source URL in internal notes |
| Calendar link in touch 1 cold | Reply-first or touch 2 after engagement |

---

## Reject the sequence if:

- Touch 1 opens with company/product name
- All three touches could send unchanged to another lead
- Proof point not traceable to product profile
- No why-now reference in touch 1
- Touch 4 planned without new trigger event

## Compliance footer (email only)

Include in founder's email signature: physical address, unsubscribe mechanism for bulk sends, honest sender identity.
