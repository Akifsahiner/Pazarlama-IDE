# Playbook: Aggressive Personalization Tiers

Spray-and-pray destroys domains. Tier 3 for top accounts.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Lead scorecard with evidence
- [ ] Offer clear (demo / waitlist / audit)
- [ ] Reply-rate benchmark set (e.g. ≥8% cold)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin research | Tier 1 only, low volume |
| standard | Good list | Tier 2 sequences |
| aggressive | Top 20 accounts | Tier 3 insights + ≤5 touches |

## Timeline
- **T-5**: Segment by tier
- **T-3**: Write tier-3 for top 20
- **H0**: Start sequence
- **D+7**: Measure reply rate

## Tactic stack
1. **`value_first_outbound_sequence`** — No pitch-first opener
2. **`outreach_tier3_personalization`** — Product-specific insight
3. Cap touches at 5; stop on reply or hard no

## Orchestration
- Depends on lead-research evidence URLs

## Realistic outcomes
- Tier 3 reply rates often 2–4× tier 1 when insight is real

## Kill / pivot rules
- Reply <2% after 40 sends → rewrite; pause volume
- Bounce >5% → list quality fail

## Ethics line
- Accurate claims only; unsubscribe honored; no purchased spam lists
