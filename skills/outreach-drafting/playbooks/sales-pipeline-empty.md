# Playbook: Sales Pipeline Empty

Value-first outbound when pipeline is zero. No pitch-first spray.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Lead scorecard with evidence URLs (from lead-research)
- [ ] Offer clear: demo / audit / waitlist
- [ ] `sales_pipeline_empty: true` — honest about starting from zero

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin research | Tier 1 only, ≤20 sends/week |
| standard | Good scorecard | Tier 2 sequences |
| aggressive | Top 20 at tier 3 | Product-specific insight, ≤5 touches |

## Timeline
- **T-5**: Segment leads by personalization tier
- **T-3**: Write tier-3 for top 20 accounts
- **H0**: Start sequence (value-first opener)
- **D+7**: Measure reply rate vs benchmark

## Tactic stack
1. **`value_first_outbound_sequence`** — Research-led opener; no pitch-first
2. **`outreach_tier3_personalization`** — Product-specific insight for top accounts
3. Cap at 5 touches; stop on reply or hard no

## Orchestration
- Depends on lead-research evidence — never draft without URL
- LinkedIn comment-before-DM when channel is LinkedIn

## Realistic outcomes
- Tier 3 reply rates often 2–4× tier 1 when insight is real
- Week 1: learning; week 2+: pattern on winning opener

## Kill / pivot rules
- Reply <2% after 40 sends → rewrite; pause volume
- Bounce >5% → list quality fail

## Ethics line
- Accurate claims only; unsubscribe honored; no purchased spam lists
