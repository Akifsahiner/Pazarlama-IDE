# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Use bulk spam tone ("Hope this finds you well", "Quick question", "Touching base"). **Why:** Instant delete; trains spam filters and burns domain reputation.
- **Don't:** Insert fake personalization tokens (`{{company}}`, `{{recent_post}}`) without filling them. **Why:** Worse than no personalization — signals automation slop.
- **Don't:** Claim "I loved your work" without citing specific work. **Why:** Generic flattery reads as template mail; reply rate <2%.
- **Don't:** Attach 5 case studies in touch 1. **Why:** Cognitive overload; first touch should fit on a phone screen.
- **Don't:** Use false urgency ("Closing 3 slots today"). **Why:** Destroys trust; especially toxic for founder-led outbound.
- **Don't:** Fabricate mutual connections or investors. **Why:** One Google search ends the relationship permanently.
- **Don't:** Send multi-paragraph essays in LinkedIn DMs. **Why:** >400 chars on mobile gets truncated unread; keep DMs under 80 words.
- **Don't:** Pitch features instead of outcome for the recipient's role. **Why:** Buyers care about their KPI, not your roadmap.
- **Don't:** Schedule or send via API from this skill. **Why:** Out of scope — drafts only; user sends manually.
- **Don't:** Reuse one email for all candidates with name swap only. **Why:** Recipients in the same community compare notes; batch sameness kills pipeline.

- **Don't:** Ship generic advice untied to this product profile. **Why:** Founders already have ChatGPT. **Cost:** Trust and retention.

- **Don't:** Recommend five channels at once. **Why:** Dilutes learning. **Cost:** No clear kill signal.
