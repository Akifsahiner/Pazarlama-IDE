# Playbook: No Audience (List Runway)

List <200 or no ESP segmentation. **Build the list and permission** before aggressive launch nurture — pair with `waitlist-hype-engine` for capture.

- **Week 1–2:** Single CTA waitlist (`waitlist-hype-engine`) — not nurture yet
- **Week 3:** Welcome email + expectation set ("We'll send build updates 2×/month")
- **Week 4:** First story email — founder problem post, no launch date
- **At 200 subscribers:** Define engaged segment manually (clicked welcome)

Do not schedule 3 launch waves until ≥200 opted-in and launch date within 30 days.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Waitlist capture live
- [ ] Consent language mentions product updates
- [ ] Honest ceiling on open rates

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | <100 emails | Welcome + monthly story only |
| standard | 100–200 | Biweekly drip, no launch waves |
| aggressive | Not yet | Upgrade at 200+ + launch date |

## Orchestration

## Realistic outcomes
- 90d to 200 quality subs with founder-led story emails
- Launch waves premature on 50-person list → 40% unsub

## Kill / pivot rules
- Bounce rate >5% → verify double opt-in
- No opens on 3 consecutive sends → shorten cadence to monthly
