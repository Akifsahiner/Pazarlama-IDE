# Case study: Launch blast at H0 → 2.1% unsub + PH hurt (lesson)

## Context
B2B SaaS. ~2.8k list (engagement unknown). Founder sent **full-list launch email at PH submit (H0)** plus tweet asking upvotes. No segmentation, no drip, no `email_ph_timing_lock`.

## What went wrong
- Violated PH organic seed window — ESP blast same minute as `ph_submit_1201_pt`
- Used subject "URGENT: vote for us on PH"
- 2.1% unsub on single send; Gmail promotions tab on domain for 3 weeks
- PH thread comments noted "spammy email blast" — social proof negative

## What should have been used
1. `email_list_hygiene_segment` — engaged-only wave 1
2. `email_ph_timing_lock` — H+6 first send
3. `email_prelaunch_drip_14d` — story before ask
4. Ethics: feedback ask, not votes

## Recovery
- Paused email 14 days
- Re-permission campaign to 800 engaged — 62% stayed
- Next launch: 2-wave sequence only → 0.4% unsub, 28% open

## Lesson
Nurture timing is part of launch mechanics — not an afterthought. Claude/Cursor-grade = coordinate ESP with PH/community clock and segment engaged first.
