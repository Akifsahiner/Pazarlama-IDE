# Case study: 6.2k engaged list + PH → 41% wave-1 open, 11% activate (anonymous)

## Context
PLG dev tool. ~6.2k waitlist (4.1k engaged by ESP rule). PH launch coordinated. Assets: founder story, demo GIF, PH gallery ready. Goal: activation spike without trashing domain rep.

## Tactic stack applied
1. `email_list_hygiene_segment` — removed 380 hard bounces; dormant 1.8k excluded from launch week
2. `email_prelaunch_drip_14d` — 5 emails over 12 days; no discounts
3. `email_subject_line_ab` — outcome subject won +18% clicks on teaser
4. `email_ph_timing_lock` — H0 no email; waves H+6 / H+9 / H+12 PT
5. `email_launch_wave_1_engaged` — 2,340 delivered engaged
6. `email_launch_wave_2_general` — 3,100 general minus dormant
7. `email_post_launch_onboarding_3` — activation checklist D+1
8. `email_teardown_metrics_d7` — shared honest stats in D+7 email

## Outcome
- Wave 1: **41% open**, 12.4% click on engaged
- Launch week: **11%** of clickers completed core activation (tracked UTM)
- Unsub launch week: **0.31%** total
- PH rank #8 — email waves did not trigger spam complaints

## Lesson
PH timing lock (H0 silent) protected both PH seed and ESP reputation. Near-miss nudge at T-3 added 140 extra engaged before launch — small but measurable.
