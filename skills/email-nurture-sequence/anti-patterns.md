# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Use cold outreach templates on waitlist subscribers. **Why:** Opt-in readers expect product story, not "quick question" LinkedIn tone.
- **Don't:** Send launch blast to entire list at PH H0. **Why:** Spam complaints hurt domain reputation; sync with `ph_submit_1201_pt` organic seed first.
- **Don't:** Fake "Re:" or "Fwd:" subject lines. **Why:** CAN-SPAM/deception risk; destroys trust with technical ICP.
- **Don't:** Six launch emails in 24 hours. **Why:** Three waves max; more triggers mass unsubscribes.
- **Don't:** Copy PH "please upvote" into ESP. **Why:** Ask for comments/feedback on product — never vote manipulation.
- **Don't:** Nurture drip that's only discounts. **Why:** Trains list to wait for promos; kills full-price conversion.
- **Don't:** Ignore dormant segment forever. **Why:** Either sunset or re-permission campaign — dormant hurts deliverability on engaged.
- **Don't:** A/B test on launch morning. **Why:** Split traffic on highest-stakes send = wasted learning.
- **Don't:** Onboarding email with 12 feature bullets. **Why:** One activation action per email (complete setup step 1).
- **Don't:** Merge waitlist referral nudge with launch wave 1 same hour. **Why:** Two emails same hour → unsub spike; sequence 48h apart.
- **Don't:** Report success with open rate only. **Why:** Track click → signup/activation; opens are vanity with Apple MPP.
- **Don't:** Use purchased or scraped lists for "nurture." **Why:** Legal and deliverability catastrophe.
- **Don't:** Skip List-Unsubscribe header. **Why:** Gmail/Yahoo bulk sender requirements (2024+).
- **Don't:** Promise "PH #1" in subject when list is cold. **Why:** Honest ceiling violation; use "we're live — early access inside."
- **Don't:** Treat B2B nurture like B2C flash sale countdown. **Why:** Dev buyers want proof and migration path, not fake scarcity timers.
