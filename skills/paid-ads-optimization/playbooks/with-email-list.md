# Playbook: Paid Ads With an Existing Email List

Your list is a conversion asset, not an ad audience to spam. Use it for seed conversions, lookalike modeling, retargeting pools, and message validation — then scale prospecting with proof.

---

## Day −14 to −7: Tracking + list hygiene

- **Day −14:** Audit conversion tracking — pixel, CAPI/server events, CRM offline conversions. No spend until demo/signup events fire in platform test tools.
- **Day −13:** Export engaged list segment (opened 2+ emails in 90 days). Remove unsubscribes, role accounts, competitors.
- **Day −12:** Hash emails for Custom Audience upload (Meta, Google Customer Match, LinkedIn Matched Audiences). Minimum 1,000 for lookalike; 100+ for retargeting.
- **Day −11:** Create dedicated LP per campaign — headline from `templates/creative-brief.md`. UTM schema locked.
- **Day −10 to −7:** Run HYP-001 on **list retargeting only** — low CPA expected; validates LP and tracking before cold spend.

---

## Day −7: Message validation via email (free insight)

- **Email subject A/B:** Send 50/50 split to power segment with two subject lines matching ad headline variants.
- **Measure:** Open + click rates predict ad CTR within ±30% if ICP-aligned.
- **Winner** becomes control headline in `templates/hypothesis-sheet.md` HYP-001.
- **Do not** send more than one A/B test email this week — list fatigue poisons retargeting pools.

---

## Day −6 to −3: Retargeting campaign live

- **Audience 1 — Hot:** Clicked email in last 30 days. Budget: 30% of total.
- **Audience 2 — Warm:** Opened 3 of last 5, no click. Budget: 20%.
- **Audience 3 — Site visitors:** Last 14 days, no conversion. Budget: 20%.
- **Creative:** Founder proof + offer. Message match to email winner.
- **Goal:** CPA ≤ 0.5× target on Audiences 1–2. If not, fix LP before prospecting.

---

## Day −2: Lookalike / similar audience seed

- **Upload** converter list: waitlist signups + email clickers + demo bookers (minimum 100, ideal 1,000+).
- **Platform:** Meta 1% LLA, Google similar segments, LinkedIn lookalike — pick primary channel per ICP (B2B → LinkedIn or Google Search; B2C → Meta/TikTok).
- **Exclude** uploaded email list from prospecting — suppress overlap to avoid paying twice for same person.
- **Budget:** 30% prospecting LLA, cap daily spend at 2× expected CPA.

---

## Day 0: Launch coordination (ads + email)

- **6am:** Retargeting ads live (Audiences 1–3).
- **7am:** LLA prospecting live with kill rules from hypothesis sheet.
- **9am:** Email to **non-converters** in retargeting pool: "Saw you checked us out — [offer]." Single send. Link to LP with same headline as ad.
- **Do not** email the full list on launch day if retargeting already covers warm segment — ESP + ad platform double-frequency annoys buyers.

---

## Day +1 to +7: Hypothesis loop

- **Daily:** Check CPA by audience tier in hypothesis sheet.
- **Day +3:** Kill variants with CPA > 2× target. Scale winners 20%.
- **Day +5:** Email **converted** users: "What almost stopped you?" — objections become ad copy variants.
- **Day +7:** First weekly review — blended CPA, audience saturation signals, next HYP-002 variable.

---

## Day +8 to +30: Scale and refresh

- **Week 2:** HYP-002 creative test on winning headline (see hypothesis roadmap).
- **Week 3:** Expand LLA to 2–3% if CPA holds; add broad prospecting only if LLA CPA ≤ target.
- **Week 4:** Refresh creative — retargeting fatigues at 3–4 weeks on same asset.
- **Email sync:** One value email per week max during ad scale; never promote same offer as display ad within 48h to same segment.

| Pool | % budget | Expected CPA vs target |
|------|----------|------------------------|

## Realistic outcomes (5–20k engaged list)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Retargeting CPA vs cold | 40–60% lower |
| LLA launch CPA | Within 1.25× target by Day 14 |
| Email-list-suppressed overlap | < 10% duplicate impressions |
| Blended CPA at Day 30 | ≤ target with 100+ conversions logged |

## Anti-patterns

- Uploading full list to LLA seed including cold/non-openers — dilutes model quality
- Running prospecting before retargeting CPA validates LP — burns budget on fixable leaks
- Same creative for email subscribers and cold traffic — warm wants proof, cold wants problem
- Daily promotional emails while retargeting ads run — frequency overload; unsubscribes spike
- No email suppression on converted users — paying to re-acquire customers

## Ethical notes

- GDPR/CCPA: list upload for ads requires consent language in privacy policy and email signup
- LinkedIn Matched Audiences: minimum audience size enforced; don't upload single contacts for stalking
- Honor opt-outs across ESP and ad platforms — sync suppressions weekly

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
