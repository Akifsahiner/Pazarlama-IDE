# Anti-Patterns

- **Don't:** Launch ads without conversion events configured and verified. **Why:** Platforms optimize for what you measure. Page-view optimization buys clicks, not customers.
- **Don't:** Split budget across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, and Twitter simultaneously with <$5k/month. **Why:** None reach statistical significance. Master one channel first.
- **Don't:** Run the same creative for 60+ days. **Why:** Creative fatigue drops CTR 30–50% by week 4. Refresh creative before tweaking bids or audiences.
- **Don't:** Scale budget 2× overnight when CPA looks good. **Why:** Algorithm resets learning phase; CPA spikes. Increase 15–20% every 3–4 days max.
- **Don't:** Send cold social traffic to a generic homepage. **Why:** Message mismatch kills conversion. Dedicated LP per campaign with matched headline and single CTA.
- **Don't:** Optimize for CPC or CTR instead of CPA/ROAS. **Why:** Cheap clicks from wrong audience are worse than expensive clicks that convert.
- **Don't:** Kill winning ad sets before 50 conversions or 7 days. **Why:** Early variance misleads. Pre-define minimum sample size in test plan; stick to it.

- **Don't:** Ship generic advice untied to this product profile. **Why:** Founders already have ChatGPT. **Cost:** Trust and retention.

- **Don't:** Recommend five channels at once. **Why:** Dilutes learning. **Cost:** No clear kill signal.

- **Don't:** Invent metrics or connector data. **Why:** Honest measurement is a product promise. **Cost:** Credibility collapse.

- **Don't:** Skip acceptance criteria on tasks. **Why:** Ambiguous done. **Cost:** Endless 'almost shipped'.

- **Don't:** Hide tradeoffs. **Why:** Senior operators state ceilings. **Cost:** Overpromise churn.
