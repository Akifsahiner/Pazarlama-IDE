## Principles

1. **Launch assets are drafts, never posts.** PH comments, tweets, emails, and ads are copy-paste ready — publishing is always user action.
2. **One positioning spine across assets.** Tagline, thread, email, and ad primary text must share the same outcome promise — no channel drift.
3. **Channel-native constraints.** PH tagline ≤60 chars; tweet thread 5–7 tweets; ad primary text ≤125 chars for Meta — enforce limits in output.
4. **Proof beats hype.** Use product profile evidence: screenshots described, real metrics, named workflows — no invented launch numbers.
5. **CTA matches channel temperature.** PH = upvote + try; email = one link; ads = benefit + action; social = conversation starter.
6. **Persona shapes tone.** Marketing persona → audience growth narrative; sales persona → revenue/outbound narrative — same product, different hook.
7. **Product type shapes format.** SaaS → demo + integration angle; consumer → identity/status; marketplace → supply/demand story.
8. **Asset bundle completeness.** Default pack: PH tagline + maker comment + tweet thread + launch email subject/body + one ad variant.
9. **Checklist for launch day.** Every pack ends with a day-of checklist (timing, supporters, reply cadence) — founders forget ops under stress.
10. **Reuse launch plan tasks.** When a launch plan exists, align asset copy to upcoming plan tasks and channels.
