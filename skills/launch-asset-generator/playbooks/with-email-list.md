# Playbook: Launch Asset Generator With an Existing Email List

Your list is the launch engine. Assets should **convert known subscribers** and give them shareable language for cold reach — not re-introduce the product from zero.

- **Email subject = best-performing past subject line variant.** Pull top open-rate subject from last 90 days; adapt for launch: "[Subject winner formula] — [product] is live." Message match lifts click→signup 20–40%.
- **Launch email structure: recognition → news → exclusive → CTA.** "You've been reading about [topic] for [months]" → "Today's the day" → "Founding member pricing until [date]" → single button. One CTA only.
- **Segment-specific email variants (2 max).** Variant A: engaged readers (full launch story). Variant B: cold subscribers (3-sentence reintro + link). Don't send 5 segments — founder can't support the replies.
- **Tweet thread assumes some followers know you.** Tweet 1 can reference the journey: "6 months ago I tweeted about [pain]. Today we're launching the fix." Still include standalone context in tweet 2 for retweet audience.
- **PH tagline unchanged — list warmth doesn't change PH cold traffic.** PH front page is 99% strangers. Tagline must pass stranger test.
- **Shareable quote tweet draft for supporters.** Give list members a pre-written post they can paste: "I've been beta testing [Product] — [specific outcome]. Live on PH today: [link]." Reduces supporter friction on launch day.
- **Ad primary text retargets site visitors only.** With list + some traffic, run retargeting ad: "You signed up for updates — [Product] is live." Cold prospecting ads wait until LP conversion proven.
- **Maker comment acknowledges community.** "Several of you from our newsletter tested this" — real if true. Links community to product without fabricating numbers.
- **Post-launch email scheduled Day +3.** "If you haven't tried it yet — here's 90 seconds on [core workflow]." Catches launch-week procrastinators.

Track: email open→click (target >25% engaged segment), supporter share count, PH upvotes attributable to list (UTM `utm_source=newsletter`).

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for launch-asset-generator
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
