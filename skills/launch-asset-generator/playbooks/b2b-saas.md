# Playbook: Launch Asset Generator for B2B SaaS

B2B launch assets sell **credibility and workflow fit** to skeptical operators — not hype to consumers. PH + LinkedIn + email + one paid variant; each channel gets native copy from one positioning spine.

- **Positioning spine = JTBD + ICP + wedge.** Example spine: "Help ops teams at Series A–C SaaS close month-end in 2 days instead of 10." Every asset inherits this; only tone and length change.
- **PH tagline: category map or outcome verb.** "Close books 5× faster for SaaS finance teams" (42 chars). Avoid "AI-powered platform for modern businesses."
- **Maker comment: problem from customer calls + demo link.** Paragraph 1: pain you heard in 10+ conversations. Paragraph 2: what it does (workflow, not features). Paragraph 3: ask ICP roles for feedback. Link to demo video or interactive tour.
- **Tweet thread slide 4 = integration or security proof.** B2B buyers scan for "works with Slack + NetSuite" or "SOC 2 Type II." One tweet dedicated to trust signals.
- **Launch email to list: ROI framing.** Subject: "[Outcome metric] without [incumbent pain]." Body: 3 bullets — time saved, risk reduced, integration fit. CTA: "Start trial" + secondary "Book 15-min with founder."
- **LinkedIn launch post (draft for founder channel).** 3 paragraphs, no "thrilled to announce." Customer quote if available; otherwise founder story with specific metric from product profile. CTA in first comment.
- **Ad primary text (LinkedIn or Google):** "[Outcome] for [ICP role]" + proof + CTA. ≤125 chars Meta / ≤150 LinkedIn intro. One variant: customer quote creative. Second variant: metric hook.
- **Sales enablement snippet in pack.** 3-bullet internal forward email the champion can send: problem, solution, proof, link. B2B launches fail when champions can't sell internally.
- **Day-of checklist: enterprise extras.** Security FAQ link ready, pricing page live, demo Calendly staffed, AE/founder reply SLA <4 hours for launch week inbound.

Track: demo/trial requests from launch window, ICP match rate on inbound (qualitative founder tag), asset constraint compliance.

## Preconditions
- [ ] Marketing profile complete for launch-asset-generator
- [ ] Primary metric named before execution
- [ ] Honest aggression dial matches real assets (list, proof, team hours)

## Aggression dial

| Level | When | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| conservative | Thin assets / solo | One channel, learning metrics |
| standard | Warm channel or list | Best-practice cadence from narrative above |
| aggressive | Strong proof + distribution | Execute full tactic stack with kill rules |

## Timeline
- **T-14**: Instrument leading metric; lock ICP + offer
- **T-7**: Ship assets referenced in narrative
- **T-1**: Final checklist + supporter warm-up
- **H0**: Primary launch motion
- **D+1…D+7**: Measure, teardown, kill or scale

## Tactic stack
1. Name bottleneck with 3 profile citations
2. Ship one measurable experiment this week
3. Tie Plan Studio tasks to registry tactic ids
4. Cap parallel channels at 2
5. Predefine kill/pivot rule before spend or blast

## Orchestration
- One primary channel from narrative; ≤2 support channels
- Repo/browser/asset tasks split by execution_mode

## Realistic outcomes
- State honest bands — do not promise #1 PH or viral without assets
- Leading metric moves in 7–14d when playbook matches profile

## Kill / pivot rules
- Leading metric misses after agreed sample → stop and rewrite hypothesis
- Never add channels when primary motion underperforms

## Ethics line
- No fake metrics, fake social proof, undisclosed ads, or ToS-breaking growth hacks
